Comprehensive Fertility & Reproductive Care

Every patient's journey is unique. We offer a full spectrum of reproductive health services delivered with clinical precision and deep human care.

Fertility Assessment

Comprehensive diagnostic testing for both partners — hormonal profiles, semen analysis, ultrasound, and genetic screening — to understand exactly what you're dealing with before charting a path forward.

Surrogacy Programme

A carefully managed, fully legal surrogacy programme connecting intended parents with compassionate carriers. We guide you through every legal, medical, and emotional step.

General Obs & Gynaecology

Full obstetric and gynaecological care — from routine check-ups to complex conditions. Our specialists treat PCOS, endometriosis, fibroids, menstrual disorders, and more.

Personalised Antenatal Care

Evidence-based antenatal monitoring tailored to each mother's needs — regular scans, nutritional counselling, risk screening, and birth preparation for a confident, safe delivery.

Intrauterine Insemination (IUI)

A less invasive first-line fertility treatment. Carefully timed sperm placement for couples with mild fertility challenges — a natural, gentle path before IVF is considered.

Step by Step

Your IVF Journey at Genesis

We demystify the process. Here's exactly what to expect from your first consultation to holding your baby.

01

Free Consultation

Meet our specialist. Share your history. We explain your options clearly — no jargon, no pressure.

02

Diagnostic Testing

Blood tests, scans, and semen analysis. We build your complete fertility profile before any treatment.

03

Stimulation & Monitoring

Carefully managed hormone stimulation to grow multiple healthy eggs, with regular scans.

04

Egg Retrieval & Fertilisation

Eggs retrieved in our lab, fertilised with your partner's or donor sperm, cultured to blastocyst stage.

05

Embryo Transfer

The best embryo transferred to your womb. Pregnancy test 10–14 days later. We celebrate with you.
