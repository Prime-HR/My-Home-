Our Services

Comprehensive Medical Care

We provide a full range of healthcare services designed to meet the needs of every patient — from routine check-ups to specialized treatment.

General Medicine

Diagnosis and treatment of common illnesses, chronic conditions, and general health concerns for adults and children.

Emergency Care

24-hour emergency services with rapid response for accidents, trauma, and acute medical conditions.

Maternity & Antenatal

Safe delivery, antenatal care, postnatal support, and neonatal care for mothers and newborns.

Laboratory Services

Full diagnostic laboratory with blood tests, urinalysis, culture and sensitivity, and rapid diagnostic kits.

Pharmacy

Well-stocked in-house pharmacy with licensed pharmacists, dispensing quality medicines at affordable prices.

Diagnostics & Imaging

Ultrasound, X-ray, ECG, and other diagnostic imaging services to support accurate and fast diagnosis.
