# Pillar 1 · Integrated Science — Lesson 38: Weather & Climate Science
### "Why the rains come, why the harmattan blows — and how we predict them"

> Paste into **NotebookLM** to generate slides/audio. **Say it** = narration/teleprompter.

**Subject:** 1.2 Integrated Science · **Lesson:** 38 · **Audience:** JHS/SHS 🇬🇭 + farmers · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Tell weather from climate. 2) Explain rain and the harmattan. 3) See how forecasting helps Ghana.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** Farmers used to read the sky. Now science reads it for the whole nation. 🌦️
**Say it:** "Will it rain for planting? Is harmattan coming? For generations, our elders read the sky. Today, science predicts weather for the whole nation — and it can make or break a farmer's harvest. Let's understand it."

## SLIDE 2 — Weather vs climate
**On-screen:** Weather = today · Climate = the usual pattern. 📅
**Say it:** "Weather is what's happening now — today's sun, rain, or wind. Climate is the usual pattern over many years — like Ghana's wet and dry seasons. Weather changes daily; climate changes slowly."

## SLIDE 3 — Where rain comes from
**On-screen:** Sun evaporates water → clouds → rain. ☁️
**Say it:** "Remember the water cycle: the sun heats water into vapour, it rises and cools into clouds, and when clouds get heavy, rain falls. So the same sun that dries your clothes also makes the rain."
**🎓 Go deeper:** Warm moist air rising and cooling forms clouds and rain."

## SLIDE 4 — The harmattan
**On-screen:** Dry, dusty winds from the Sahara. 🌬️
**Say it:** "Each dry season, the harmattan brings cool, dry, dusty winds down from the Sahara desert. It cracks lips and dusts everything — a clear example of how distant land and wind shape our local weather."

## SLIDE 5 — Forecasting saves lives & harvests
**On-screen:** Predicting floods, droughts, planting times. 🌾
**Say it:** "Scientists use instruments and satellites to forecast the weather. This warns of floods, guides farmers on when to plant, and helps everyone prepare. Good forecasting is real protection for lives and livelihoods."

## SLIDE 6 — Climate change shifts it all
**On-screen:** Patterns are becoming less reliable. ⚠️
**Say it:** "As we learned, climate change is disturbing these patterns — rains arrive late, droughts last longer, storms grow stronger. Understanding weather science helps Ghana adapt and plan for a changing future."

## SLIDE 7 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Read the sky with science. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: weather is now, climate is the long pattern; rain comes from the sun, vapour, and clouds; the harmattan blows from the Sahara; forecasting protects farmers and lives; climate change is shifting it all. Challenge: watch the sky and a forecast for two days and compare."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Weather (now) vs climate (pattern) · 2) Sun→vapour→clouds→rain · 3) Forecasting protects Ghana.
