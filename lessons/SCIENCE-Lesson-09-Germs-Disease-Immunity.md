# Pillar 1 · Integrated Science — Lesson 9: Germs, Disease & Your Immune System
### "Tiny invaders you can't see — and the army inside you that fights them"

> Paste into **NotebookLM** to generate slides/audio. **Say it** = narration/teleprompter.

**Subject:** 1.2 Integrated Science · **Lesson:** 9 · **Audience:** Everyone 🇬🇭 + JHS · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Know what germs are. 2) Understand how the body defends itself. 3) Prevent common diseases like malaria.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** The deadliest things in Ghana are too small to see. 🦠
**Say it:** "Malaria, cholera, typhoid — the things that send people to hospital are tiny germs invisible to the eye. But you have a powerful army inside you to fight them. Let's learn both the enemy and your defence."

## SLIDE 2 — What are germs?
**On-screen:** Bacteria, viruses, parasites. 🔬
**Say it:** "Germs are tiny living things that cause disease — bacteria (like those in dirty water), viruses (like flu and COVID), and parasites (like the malaria one carried by mosquitoes)."

## SLIDE 3 — How germs spread
**On-screen:** Dirty hands · water · air · mosquitoes. ✋
**Say it:** "Germs travel by dirty hands, unclean water and food, coughs in the air, and bites from mosquitoes. Knowing how they spread tells you exactly how to block them."

## SLIDE 4 — Your immune system
**On-screen:** White blood cells = your soldiers. 🛡️
**Say it:** "Your body has a defence force — white blood cells that find and destroy germs. A well-fed, rested body has a strong army; a weak, hungry one falls sick easily."
**🎓 Go deeper:** Fighting germs is the job of the **immune system**."

## SLIDE 5 — Vaccines: training the army
**On-screen:** A vaccine teaches your body to win in advance. 💉
**Say it:** "A vaccine shows your immune system a harmless piece of a germ, so it learns to defeat the real one fast. That's how we beat polio and measles. Vaccines are science protecting children."

## SLIDE 6 — Prevention beats cure
**On-screen:** Wash hands · clean water · nets · vaccines. 🧼
**Say it:** "Most disease here is preventable. Wash hands, drink clean water, sleep under treated nets against malaria, keep surroundings clean, and take your vaccines. Cheap habits, lives saved."

## SLIDE 7 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Block the germ, back your army. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: germs are tiny disease-causers; they spread by hands, water, air, and mosquitoes; your immune system fights them; vaccines train it; prevention is best. Challenge: name how each common disease spreads, and block one route today."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Germs = bacteria/viruses/parasites · 2) Immune system defends · 3) Prevent: hygiene, nets, vaccines.

## Quiz
