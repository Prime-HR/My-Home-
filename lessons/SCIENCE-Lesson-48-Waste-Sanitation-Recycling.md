# Pillar 1 · Integrated Science — Lesson 48: Waste, Sanitation & Recycling
### "Your rubbish doesn't disappear — and that's both a problem and an opportunity"

> Paste into **NotebookLM** to generate slides/audio. **Say it** = narration/teleprompter.

**Subject:** 1.2 Integrated Science · **Lesson:** 48 · **Audience:** Everyone 🇬🇭 + JHS/SHS · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Understand waste and sanitation. 2) Know the dangers of poor waste handling. 3) See recycling as opportunity.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** The plastic you throw today may still be here in 400 years. ♻️
**Say it:** "When you drop a sachet or bottle, where does it go? Often into our gutters, beaches, and the sea — for centuries. Poor waste causes the floods and disease we suffer. But waste can also become wealth. Let's see how."

## SLIDE 2 — Where waste comes from
**On-screen:** Homes, markets, factories, plastic. 🗑️
**Say it:** "We produce waste daily — food scraps, plastic, paper, metal. Plastic is the biggest headache because it doesn't rot away; it just breaks into smaller and smaller pieces."

## SLIDE 3 — Poor waste = disease & floods
**On-screen:** Blocked gutters, dirty water, mosquitoes. 🦟
**Say it:** "Rubbish thrown carelessly blocks gutters, causing the floods that hit Accra. It breeds mosquitoes and germs, spreading malaria and cholera. Clean surroundings are a science-backed shield for health."

## SLIDE 4 — Sanitation
**On-screen:** Clean toilets, clean water, clean hands. 🚽
**Say it:** "Sanitation means safely managing human waste and keeping clean. Proper toilets, clean water, and handwashing break the chain of disease. It's one of the greatest life-savers in history."

## SLIDE 5 — The 3 Rs & turning waste to wealth
**On-screen:** Reduce · Reuse · Recycle. ♻️💰
**Say it:** "Three powerful words: REDUCE what you use, REUSE what you can, RECYCLE the rest. Across Ghana, people now turn plastic into pavement blocks, bags, and crafts — earning money while cleaning the nation."
**🎓 Go deeper:** Turning waste back into useful products is the **circular economy**."

## SLIDE 6 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Clean Ghana is a healthy, wealthy Ghana. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: waste, especially plastic, lasts a long time; poor handling causes floods and disease; sanitation saves lives; and the 3 Rs turn waste into wealth. Challenge: reduce or reuse one piece of plastic today, and never litter."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Waste lasts (esp. plastic) · 2) Poor waste = floods & disease · 3) Reduce, reuse, recycle.
