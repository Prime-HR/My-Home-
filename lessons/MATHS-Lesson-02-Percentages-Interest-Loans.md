## SLIDE 1 — The hook
**On-screen:** That quick loan app charged you 30%… do you know what that really costs? 📱💸
**Say it:** "Don't scroll. A loan can rescue your business or swallow it whole — and the only difference is whether you understood the percentages. Today, the maths that keeps you out of debt traps."

## SLIDE 2 — Percentage made simple
**On-screen:** "Per cent" = "out of 100." 🔢
**Say it:** "Percentage just means 'out of 100'. 25% means 25 out of every 100. To find 25% of 200 cedis: divide by 100, times 25 — that's 50 cedis. Master this one move and money stops confusing you."
**🎓 Go deeper (students):** % of an amount = (amount ÷ 100) × the percent.

## SLIDE 3 — Interest: rent on money
**On-screen:** Interest = the extra you pay to borrow. 🏦
**Say it:** "When you borrow, you pay back more than you took. That extra is interest — like paying rent on the money. Borrow 1,000 cedis at 20% interest, you repay 1,200. The 200 is the cost of the loan."

## SLIDE 4 — The hidden danger: the time trap
**On-screen:** "30% per month" ≠ "30% per year." ⏰
**Say it:** "Here's the trap that ruins people. '30%' sounds small — but 30% PER MONTH is huge. Over a year that's far more than the goods are worth. Always ask: percent per month or per year? The time word changes everything."

## SLIDE 5 — Compound interest: snowball
**On-screen:** Interest on top of interest grows fast. ❄️➡️⛄
**Say it:** "Sometimes interest is charged on what you already owe, including past interest. It snowballs. This is how a small debt becomes a mountain — and, on the good side, how savings grow if YOU are the one earning the interest."

## SLIDE 6 — Make it work FOR you
**On-screen:** Save & invest → interest pays YOU. 📈
**Say it:** "Flip it. Put money in a savings account or a good investment, and that same interest works in your favour. The earlier you start, the more the snowball grows. Time is the saver's best friend."

## SLIDE 7 — Before you borrow: 3 questions
**On-screen:** How much extra? Per month/year? Can I repay? ✅
**Say it:** "Before any loan, ask three things. One: how much total will I pay back? Two: is the rate per month or per year? Three: can I really repay on time? If the maths scares you, that's your answer."

## SLIDE 8 — Why it matters
**On-screen:** Numbers keep you free. 🔓
**Say it:** "Lenders count on people not doing the maths. Do the maths, and no app, no lender, no 'susu' trick can trap you. This is financial freedom in one skill."

## SLIDE 9 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Count before you commit. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: percent means out of 100; interest is the cost of borrowing; watch the time word; let interest work for you by saving. Challenge: find the real total cost of one loan offer you've seen — do the full maths before you ever take it."
**Recap in 3:** 1) % = out of 100 · 2) Interest = cost of borrowing (watch per month vs year) · 3) Saving makes interest pay YOU.

## Quick Quiz
1. What is 10% of ₵350? *(₵35.)*
2. Why is "30% per month" dangerous? *(It's far more than 30% per year — it adds up fast.)*
3. How can interest work FOR you? *(By saving/investing so you earn it.)*

## Teacher version (45 min)
- 0–5: "Has a loan ever cost more than expected?" Discuss.
- 5–18: Teach percentages and simple interest with examples.
- 18–30: Compare two loan offers; calculate true totals.
- 30–40: Show saving with interest over time.
- 40–45: Each writes the 3 questions to ask before borrowing.
