# Pillar 1 · Integrated Science — Lesson 7: Water — The Science of Life
### "The same water can give life or take it — the science decides which"

> Paste into **NotebookLM** to generate slides/audio. **Say it** = narration/teleprompter.

**Subject:** 1.2 Integrated Science · **Lesson:** 7 · **Audience:** Everyone 🇬🇭 + JHS · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Explain the water cycle. 2) Know why clean water matters. 3) Name simple ways to make water safe.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** Dirty water kills more people than many wars. The science can stop it. 💧
**Say it:** "Water gives life — but dirty water spreads cholera, typhoid, and bilharzia that harm thousands. The good news: simple science makes water safe. This lesson can literally save lives."

## SLIDE 2 — The water cycle
**On-screen:** Evaporate → cloud → rain → rivers → repeat. 🔁
**Say it:** "Water is never new — it cycles. The sun evaporates water from rivers and the sea; it forms clouds; it falls as rain; it flows back to rivers and the ground. The same water has been recycling for millions of years."
**🎓 Go deeper:** Evaporation, condensation, precipitation — the three steps."

## SLIDE 3 — Water and your body
**On-screen:** Your body is mostly water. 🫗
**Say it:** "More than half of you is water. It carries nutrients, cools you, and removes waste. That's why drinking enough clean water every day keeps you alert and healthy."

## SLIDE 4 — When water becomes the enemy
**On-screen:** Germs in dirty water cause disease. 🦠
**Say it:** "Water from a dirty source can carry germs that cause cholera, typhoid, and diarrhoea — major killers, especially of children. Galamsey poisoning our rivers makes this worse."

## SLIDE 5 — Make it safe
**On-screen:** Boil · filter · treat · store clean. 🔥
**Say it:** "Simple science makes water safe: boil it, filter it, use treatment, and store it covered and clean. These cheap steps prevent most water-borne disease."

## SLIDE 6 — Protect our water
**On-screen:** Stop galamsey, stop dumping. 🚱
**Say it:** "Ghana is blessed with the Volta and many rivers. Protecting them from galamsey and waste isn't only environmental — it's about keeping our drinking water alive for the future."

## SLIDE 7 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Clean water = life. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: water cycles endlessly; your body needs it; dirty water spreads disease; boiling and filtering make it safe; protect our rivers. Challenge: make sure your drinking water is treated, and teach one family the safe steps."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Water cycles · 2) Dirty water = disease · 3) Boil/filter/treat to be safe.

## Quiz
1. Name the 3 steps of the water cycle. *(Evaporation, condensation, precipitation.)* 2. Name a water-borne disease. *(Cholera, typhoid, diarrhoea.)* 3. Name one way to make water safe. *(Boil, filter, treat.)*
