# Pillar 1 · Integrated Science — Lesson 55: Energy Efficiency at Home
### "The science of a lower light bill — without sitting in darkness"

**Subject:** 1.2 Integrated Science · **Lesson:** 55 · **Audience:** Everyone 🇬🇭 + JHS · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Understand energy waste. 2) Use efficient choices. 3) Cut bills and pollution.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** Two homes, same size — one pays double. The difference is science. 💡
**Say it:** "Two families live in similar houses, but one's light bill is twice the other's. They're not richer — they're smarter about energy. Let's learn the simple science that keeps cedis in your pocket."

## SLIDE 2 — Energy costs money
**On-screen:** Every appliance turns electricity into a bill. 🔌
**Say it:** "Remember the electricity lesson — every device draws energy you pay for. Some quietly eat far more than others. Knowing which lets you control your bill instead of being shocked by it."

## SLIDE 3 — The big eaters
**On-screen:** Irons, heaters, old fridges, AC. 🔥
**Say it:** "The biggest energy users make heat or cold — electric irons, water heaters, old fridges, and air conditioners. Using them wisely makes the biggest difference to your bill."

## SLIDE 4 — Efficient choices
**On-screen:** LED bulbs · efficient appliances · switch off. 💡
**Say it:** "Swap old bulbs for LED ones that give the same light for a fraction of the power. Choose energy-efficient appliances. And switch off and unplug what you're not using — devices on standby still sip power."
**🎓 Go deeper:** Energy-rating labels show how efficient an appliance is — check before you buy."

## SLIDE 5 — Use nature for free
**On-screen:** Daylight, ventilation, the sun. ☀️
**Say it:** "Free energy is all around: open curtains for daylight instead of bulbs, let breeze cool a room before reaching for the fan, and dry clothes in the sun. Working with nature cuts the bill to nothing."

## SLIDE 6 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Save energy, save money, save the planet. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: every device costs money; heaters and coolers use the most; LEDs and efficient appliances and switching off save a lot; and nature gives free light and air. Challenge: find your home's biggest energy waster and fix it this week."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Devices cost money · 2) LEDs + efficient + switch off · 3) Use free daylight & breeze.

## Quiz
1. Name two appliances that use lots of energy. *(Iron, heater, old fridge, AC.)* 2. Why switch to LED bulbs? *(Same light, far less power.)* 3. Name one free, natural energy source at home. *(Daylight, breeze, sun.)*

## Teacher (45 min)
List home appliances → which use most → efficient swaps (LED) → standby power → using daylight/breeze → find-the-waster homework.
