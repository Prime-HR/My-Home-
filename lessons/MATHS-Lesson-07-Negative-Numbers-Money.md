# Pillar 1 · Mathematics — Lesson 7: Negative Numbers & Money
### "Below zero is real — it's the difference between profit and debt"

> Paste into **NotebookLM** to generate slides/audio. **Say it** = narration/teleprompter.

**Subject:** 1.1 Mathematics · **Lesson:** 7 · **Audience:** Everyone 🇬🇭 + JHS/SHS · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Understand negative numbers. 2) Add and subtract them. 3) See them in money, debt, and temperature.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** "I'm not at zero — I'm BELOW zero." That's debt. 📉
**Say it:** "When you owe more than you have, you're not at zero — you're below it. Negative numbers describe debt, loss, and cold. Understand them and you understand your true money position."

## SLIDE 2 — The number line
**On-screen:** …−3 −2 −1 0 1 2 3… ↔️
**Say it:** "Picture a line. Zero in the middle. To the right, positive — money you have. To the left, negative — money you owe. Everything lives on this line."

## SLIDE 3 — Negatives in real life
**On-screen:** Debt · loss · cold temperature · overdraft. 🧊
**Say it:** "Negatives are everywhere: a 20-cedi debt is −20, a business loss is negative profit, freezing temperatures go below zero, a bank overdraft is negative balance."

## SLIDE 4 — Adding & subtracting
**On-screen:** Owe ₵50, pay ₵30 → still owe ₵20 (−50 + 30 = −20). ➕➖
**Say it:** "If you owe 50 (that's −50) and pay back 30 (+30), you still owe 20 (−20). Adding a positive to a negative moves you toward zero — toward freedom."

## SLIDE 5 — Two negatives, careful!
**On-screen:** Taking away a debt makes you richer. 💡
**Say it:** "Here's the tricky part people love: if someone cancels a 40-cedi debt you owed, removing that −40 actually leaves you 40 better off. Taking away a negative is a positive."
**🎓 Go deeper:** Subtracting a negative = adding a positive (− (−40) = +40)."

## SLIDE 6 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Know your real position. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: negatives sit left of zero; they show debt, loss, and cold; adding positives moves you toward zero; removing a debt makes you richer. Challenge: write your true money position today — what you have minus what you owe."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Negative = below zero (debt/loss) · 2) Add positives to climb to zero · 3) Removing a debt helps you.

## Quiz
1. You owe ₵80 and pay ₵50 — what's your balance? *(−₵30.)* 2. Is a business loss positive or negative profit? *(Negative.)* 3. −(−20) = ? *(+20.)*

## Teacher (45 min)
Draw a number line on the floor; students step left/right for debt/payment → real debt examples → add/subtract practice → "true position" homework.
