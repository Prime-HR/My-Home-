# Pillar 1 · Integrated Science — Lesson 6: Plants & Photosynthesis
### "Green leaves are tiny factories feeding the whole world — including you"

> Paste into **NotebookLM** to generate slides/audio. **Say it** = narration/teleprompter.

**Subject:** 1.2 Integrated Science · **Lesson:** 6 · **Audience:** Everyone 🇬🇭 + JHS · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Explain photosynthesis simply. 2) Name what plants give us. 3) See why protecting plants protects life.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** Every meal you eat started with sunlight on a leaf. ☀️🌿
**Say it:** "Cocoa, maize, the air you breathe — trace them all back and you reach a green leaf catching sunlight. Plants are the silent factories feeding all life. Let's see the magic inside the leaf."

## SLIDE 2 — Photosynthesis in one line
**On-screen:** Sunlight + water + air → food + oxygen. 🔆
**Say it:** "Green plants take sunlight, water from the soil, and carbon dioxide from the air, and turn them into food (sugar) and oxygen. That process is called photosynthesis — making food from light."
**🎓 Go deeper:** The green substance that catches light is **chlorophyll**."

## SLIDE 3 — Plants feed everything
**On-screen:** Plants → animals → us. The food chain. 🔗
**Say it:** "Plants are the base of every food chain. We eat plants directly, or we eat animals that ate plants. No plants, no food — for anyone."

## SLIDE 4 — Plants give us oxygen
**On-screen:** They breathe out the air we breathe in. 🌬️
**Say it:** "While making food, plants release oxygen — the gas we need to live. Forests are the lungs of the Earth. Every tree cut down is a breath taken from the future."

## SLIDE 5 — Ghana grows on this
**On-screen:** Cocoa, maize, cassava, yam — our economy. 🇬🇭
**Say it:** "Ghana's farms — cocoa, maize, cassava, plantain — are photosynthesis at work, feeding families and earning the nation money. Understanding plant science makes better farmers."

## SLIDE 6 — Protect the green
**On-screen:** Trees = food, air, rain, shade. 🌳
**Say it:** "Protecting plants isn't only about beauty. Trees bring rain, cool the air, hold the soil, and feed us. The galamsey and deforestation that destroy them harm everyone."

## SLIDE 7 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Thank a leaf today. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: photosynthesis turns sunlight, water, and air into food and oxygen; plants feed every food chain and give us air; Ghana's economy grows on them. Challenge: plant one seed or tree, and watch the factory start."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Sunlight+water+air → food+oxygen · 2) Plants feed all life · 3) Protect them.

## Quiz
1. What does photosynthesis make? *(Food/sugar and oxygen.)* 2. What gas do plants release? *(Oxygen.)* 3. What green substance catches light? *(Chlorophyll.)*

Look at a leaf → teach photosynthesis → draw a food chain → discuss oxygen & forests → plant a seed activity.
