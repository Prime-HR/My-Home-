# AI For Ghana — Lesson 9: How to Use AI as a Student
### "Your free 24/7 tutor for BECE & WASSCE — used the RIGHT way"

3. Build a simple AI study routine for exams.

## SLIDE 1 — The hook
**On-screen:** A free private tutor for every subject — in your pocket. 📚🤖
**Say it:** "Rich kids pay for extra classes. But you have something just as powerful and completely free — if you use it right. AI can be your private tutor for every WASSCE and BECE subject. Here's how."

## SLIDE 2 — One line
**On-screen:** Use AI to LEARN faster — never to cheat. 🎯
**Say it:** "One golden rule first: AI is to help you learn, not to do your work for you. Use it to understand, and you'll fly. Use it to cheat, and you'll fail real life."

## SLIDE 3 — Explain hard topics simply
**On-screen:** "Explain [topic] like I'm 12, with a Ghana example." 🧠
**Say it:** "Stuck on a topic? Ask AI to explain it simply, step by step, with a local example. 'Explain electrolysis like I'm 12, using a Ghana example.' Suddenly the hard thing makes sense."

## SLIDE 4 — Make summaries & notes
**On-screen:** Turn long notes into short, clear revision points. 📝
**Say it:** "Paste a long passage and ask for a short summary or key points. Great for fast revision the night before a test — but always read the original too."

## SLIDE 5 — Quiz yourself
**On-screen:** "Ask me 10 questions on this topic, then mark me." ✅
**Say it:** "This is gold. Ask AI to test you with questions, answer them, then ask it to mark you and explain your mistakes. Testing yourself is the #1 proven way to remember."

## SLIDE 6 — Improve your writing
**On-screen:** Get feedback on YOUR essay — don't copy its essay. ✍️
**Say it:** "Write your own essay, then ask AI for feedback — grammar, structure, stronger points. You learn and improve. Copying its essay wholesale teaches you nothing and examiners can tell."

## SLIDE 7 — The cheating line
**On-screen:** Learn WITH AI ✅ · Submit AI's work as yours ❌
**Say it:** "Be clear and honest. Using AI to understand, practise, and improve — that's smart studying. Submitting AI's answers as your own — that's cheating yourself out of an education you'll need for life."
**🎓 Go deeper:** Doing your own work honestly is called **academic integrity**.

## SLIDE 8 — A simple AI study routine
**On-screen:** Explain → Summarise → Self-test → Fix mistakes. 🔁
**Say it:** "Try this loop for any topic: ask AI to explain it, then summarise it, then quiz you, then explain your mistakes. Twenty focused minutes can beat two passive hours."

## SLIDE 9 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Your free tutor is ready. ✅ Use it tonight.
**Say it:** "Recap: explain, summarise, self-test, get feedback — and never just copy. Challenge: tonight, pick one hard topic and run the study loop with AI. Then teach a classmate."
**Recap in 3:** 1) AI = tutor, not cheat-sheet · 2) Explain, summarise, self-test · 3) Do your own work.

## Teacher version (45 min)
- 0–5: "What's your hardest subject?" Collect topics.
- 5–18: Demo the study loop live on a hard topic.
- 18–30: Students run the loop on their own topic (phones/lab).
- 30–40: Discuss the cheating line and integrity.
- 40–45: Each commits to one AI study habit.
