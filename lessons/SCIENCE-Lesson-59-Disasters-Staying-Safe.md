# Pillar 1 · Integrated Science — Lesson 59: Natural Disasters & Staying Safe
### "Floods, fires, and storms — the science that helps you prepare and survive"

**Subject:** 1.2 Integrated Science · **Lesson:** 59 · **Audience:** Everyone 🇬🇭 + JHS/SHS · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Understand common hazards in Ghana. 2) Know preparation and safety steps. 3) Reduce risk for family and community.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** The Accra floods kill almost every year — but much of it is preventable. 🌊
**Say it:** "Floods, fires, storms, and disease outbreaks strike Ghana, often with warning we ignore. Science helps us predict, prepare, and survive. This lesson could one day protect your family. Let's get ready."

## SLIDE 2 — Floods
**On-screen:** Blocked drains + heavy rain = danger. 🌧️
**Say it:** "We learned how blocked gutters cause floods. Stay safe: don't build or dump in waterways, heed weather warnings, move to higher ground early, and never cross fast-moving flood water — it's stronger than it looks."

## SLIDE 3 — Fire
**On-screen:** Electrical faults, gas, candles, careless flames. 🔥
**Say it:** "Most fires are preventable: don't overload sockets, turn off gas and irons, keep flames away from cloth, and never leave cooking unattended. Know two ways out of any building and a meeting point outside."

## SLIDE 4 — Storms & lightning
**On-screen:** Shelter properly; avoid tall isolated trees. ⛈️
**Say it:** "In storms, get indoors. Avoid sheltering under tall lone trees or in open fields during lightning, and unplug sensitive electronics. Respect the power of the weather."

## SLIDE 5 — Be prepared
**On-screen:** Plan · emergency kit · know the numbers. 🎒
**Say it:** "Preparation saves lives. Agree a family plan and meeting point, keep a simple emergency kit (torch, water, basic first aid), and save emergency numbers. Calm, prepared people survive and help others."

## SLIDE 6 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Prepared today, safe tomorrow. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: floods come from blocked drains and heavy rain; fires from carelessness; storms demand proper shelter; and preparation — a plan, a kit, the numbers — saves lives. Challenge: make a simple family emergency plan and a meeting point."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Don't block waterways; avoid flood water · 2) Prevent fire; know exits · 3) Have a plan + kit + numbers.

## Quiz
1. What causes much of Accra's flooding? *(Blocked drains plus heavy rain.)* 2. Name one fire-prevention step. *(Don't overload sockets; don't leave cooking; keep flames from cloth.)* 3. Name one item in an emergency kit. *(Torch, water, first aid.)*

List local hazards → floods, fire, storms → prevention & safety steps → build a family plan & kit list → practise an exit route.
