## SLIDE 1 — The hook
**On-screen:** You sell every day — but do you KNOW your real profit? 💰🤔
**Say it:** "Be honest. You buy and sell — but if I asked your exact profit on one item today, could you tell me? Most traders can't. And that's why some work hard every day yet stay broke. Today, the maths that protects your money."

## SLIDE 2 — One line
**On-screen:** Maths isn't the enemy. It's the language of money. 🗣️
**Say it:** "Forget exam fear. Maths is simply the language of money — and whoever speaks it well keeps more of it."

## SLIDE 3 — Cost vs Selling price
**On-screen:** What you PAID vs what you SELL for. 🏷️
**Say it:** "Two numbers rule everything. The cost price — what you paid for the goods. The selling price — what the customer pays you. Get these clear in your head for every item."
**🎓 Go deeper (students):** Cost price (CP) and Selling price (SP) — the foundation of all money maths.

## SLIDE 4 — Profit = Selling − Cost
**On-screen:** Sell ₵150, it cost ₵100 → ₵50 profit. ➖
**Say it:** "Profit is just selling price minus cost price. You bought a basket of tomatoes for 100 cedis and sold everything for 150 — your profit is 50 cedis. Simple. Do this for every product you sell."

## SLIDE 5 — The hidden costs trap
**On-screen:** Transport · spoilage · data · time. Subtract them too! ⚠️
**Say it:** "Here's where many fool themselves. That 50 cedis 'profit' — did you remove transport to the market? The tomatoes that spoiled? Your data and time? Real profit subtracts ALL costs. Sometimes what felt like profit was actually a small loss."

## SLIDE 6 — Percentages: markup & discount
**On-screen:** "20% off" and "add 30%" — know what they mean. 🔢
**Say it:** "Percentages are everywhere. To add 30% markup on a 100-cedi item: 100 plus 30 is 130. A '20% discount' on 50 cedis: 20% of 50 is 10, so you pay 40. Knowing this stops you being cheated and helps you price right."
**🎓 Go deeper (students):** Profit margin % = (profit ÷ cost) × 100.

## SLIDE 7 — Mental math at the market
**On-screen:** Quick checks = nobody cheats you on change. 🧮
**Say it:** "Train quick mental maths for change and totals. The faster you calculate, the harder it is for anyone to short-change you — and the more confident you look to customers."

## SLIDE 8 — Why it matters
**On-screen:** Numbers protect you and grow you. 📈
**Say it:** "This same maths handles your MoMo, your loans, the interest a lender charges, your savings. People who master money maths build wealth. People who avoid it stay at the mercy of others."

## SLIDE 9 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Know your numbers, keep your money. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: profit is selling minus cost; always subtract hidden costs; use percentages for markup and discounts. Challenge: pick ONE thing you sell and calculate its true profit today — including transport and spoilage. The number may shock you."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Profit = SP − CP · 2) Subtract ALL hidden costs · 3) Master percentages.

## Quick Quiz
