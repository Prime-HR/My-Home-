# Pillar 1 · English Language — Lesson 5: Grammar 1 — Parts of Speech
### "Every sentence is built from 8 kinds of words — know them and you control language"

**Objectives:** 1) Name the main parts of speech. 2) Spot them in a sentence. 3) Use them to build clearer English.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** Every sentence you've ever spoken used just 8 types of words. 🧩
**Say it:** "Language can feel like a mystery, but every sentence is built from only eight kinds of words — like eight Lego bricks. Learn the bricks and you can build anything. Let's master them simply."

## SLIDE 2 — Nouns & pronouns
**On-screen:** Noun = a name · Pronoun = stands in for it. 🏷️
**Say it:** "A NOUN names something — Kofi, Accra, market, joy. A PRONOUN replaces it so you don't repeat — he, she, it, they. 'Kofi went to the market; HE bought rice.'"

## SLIDE 3 — Verbs & adverbs
**On-screen:** Verb = action · Adverb = how/when. 🏃
**Say it:** "A VERB is doing or being — run, sell, is, eat. An ADVERB tells how, when, or where the action happens — quickly, yesterday, here. 'She sells quickly.'"
**🎓 Go deeper:** Every complete sentence needs at least a noun and a verb."

## SLIDE 4 — Adjectives
**On-screen:** Adjective = describes a noun. 🎨
**Say it:** "An ADJECTIVE describes a noun — big, sweet, red, busy. 'The busy market' or 'sweet mango'. Adjectives bring your writing and speaking to life."

## SLIDE 5 — The connectors
**On-screen:** Prepositions · conjunctions · interjections. 🔗
**Say it:** "The smaller helpers: PREPOSITIONS show position — in, on, under, to. CONJUNCTIONS join — and, but, because. INTERJECTIONS show feeling — wow! oh! Together they glue sentences."

## SLIDE 6 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Know the bricks, build any sentence. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: nouns name, pronouns replace, verbs act, adverbs modify actions, adjectives describe, and prepositions, conjunctions, and interjections connect and express. Challenge: in one sentence, point out the noun, verb, and adjective."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Noun/pronoun, verb/adverb, adjective · 2) Prepositions/conjunctions connect · 3) Noun + verb = a sentence.

## Quiz
1. What does a verb do? *(Shows action or being.)* 2. What does an adjective do? *(Describes a noun.)* 3. Give a pronoun. *(He, she, it, they…)*
