# Pillar 1 · Integrated Science — Lesson 8: Air, Breathing & Pollution
### "You can live weeks without food, days without water — but minutes without air"

> Paste into **NotebookLM** to generate slides/audio. **Say it** = narration/teleprompter.

**Subject:** 1.2 Integrated Science · **Lesson:** 8 · **Audience:** Everyone 🇬🇭 + JHS · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Say what air is made of. 2) Explain breathing and why we need oxygen. 3) Understand air pollution and how to reduce it.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** The most important thing you'll use today is invisible. 🌬️
**Say it:** "You can survive weeks without food and days without water — but only minutes without air. This invisible thing keeps you alive every second. Let's understand it, and how we're harming it."

## SLIDE 2 — What is air?
**On-screen:** Mostly nitrogen, plus the oxygen we need. 💨
**Say it:** "Air is a mix of gases — mostly nitrogen, about a fifth oxygen, and small amounts of others like carbon dioxide. Oxygen is the part your body must have."

## SLIDE 3 — Breathing
**On-screen:** Lungs take in oxygen, release carbon dioxide. 🫁
**Say it:** "When you breathe in, your lungs pull oxygen into your blood, which carries it to every cell for energy. When you breathe out, you release carbon dioxide — the waste gas. In, out, all day, without you thinking."
**🎓 Go deeper:** Using oxygen to release energy in cells is **respiration**."

## SLIDE 4 — Plants and air balance
**On-screen:** Plants take CO₂, give oxygen — we do the reverse. ♻️
**Say it:** "Beautifully, plants do the opposite of us — they take in carbon dioxide and give out oxygen. Humans and plants keep the air in balance. That's why forests matter for the very air we breathe."

## SLIDE 5 — Air pollution
**On-screen:** Smoke, fumes, dust harm your lungs. 🏭
**Say it:** "Burning rubbish, vehicle fumes, and dust dirty our air. Breathing polluted air causes coughs, asthma, and long-term lung disease — a growing problem in busy cities like Accra."

## SLIDE 6 — Cleaner air, healthier life
**On-screen:** Don't burn waste · plant trees · cleaner fuel. 🌳
**Say it:** "We can protect our air: avoid burning plastic and rubbish, plant trees, support cleaner transport and cooking. Clean air is a right worth fighting for."

## SLIDE 7 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Guard the air you breathe. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: air is mostly nitrogen with vital oxygen; breathing brings oxygen in and carbon dioxide out; plants balance the air; pollution harms our lungs. Challenge: avoid burning rubbish today, and notice your breathing."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Oxygen keeps you alive · 2) Plants balance the air · 3) Stop pollution.

## Quiz
1. Which gas in air does your body need? *(Oxygen.)* 2. What gas do you breathe out? *(Carbon dioxide.)* 3. Name one cause of air pollution. *(Burning rubbish, vehicle fumes, dust.)*

## Teacher (45 min)
Hold breath briefly to feel the need for air → teach air composition + breathing → plant/human balance → discuss local pollution → clean-air pledge.
