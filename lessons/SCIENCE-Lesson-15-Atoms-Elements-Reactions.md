# Pillar 1 · Integrated Science — Lesson 15: Atoms, Elements & Reactions
### "Everything — you, water, gold, air — is built from tiny invisible bricks"

**Subject:** 1.2 Integrated Science · **Lesson:** 15 · **Audience:** Everyone 🇬🇭 + JHS/SHS · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Understand atoms and elements. 2) Know what a chemical reaction is. 3) Spot reactions in daily life.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** Break anything down far enough and you reach the same tiny bricks. 🧱
**Say it:** "Your body, water, gold, the air — all made of the same kind of invisible building blocks called atoms. Understanding them is understanding what everything is made of. Let's go small to understand the big."

## SLIDE 2 — Atoms: the tiny bricks
**On-screen:** Atoms = the smallest pieces of matter. ⚛️
**Say it:** "Atoms are unimaginably tiny — millions would fit on the tip of a pin. Everything you can touch is built from them, arranged in different ways."

## SLIDE 3 — Elements
**On-screen:** One type of atom = an element (oxygen, gold, iron). 🥇
**Say it:** "When all the atoms are the same type, we call it an element — like pure gold, oxygen gas, or iron. There are about a hundred basic elements, and they combine to make everything else."
**🎓 Go deeper:** Atoms join to form **molecules** — e.g., water is two hydrogen + one oxygen (H₂O)."

## SLIDE 4 — Chemical reactions
**On-screen:** Atoms rearrange → a new substance forms. 🔄
**Say it:** "A chemical reaction is when atoms rearrange to make something new. Burning wood, rusting iron, cooking an egg, fermenting banku — the starting things change into different substances. New stuff is created."

## SLIDE 5 — Reactions all around you
**On-screen:** Rust, fire, cooking, digestion, batteries. 🔋
**Say it:** "Reactions run your life. Iron rusts in the rain, fire releases energy, your stomach digests food with chemistry, a battery powers your torch through reactions. Chemistry isn't a lab — it's everywhere."

## SLIDE 6 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Know what the world is made of. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: atoms are the tiny bricks of everything; one type of atom is an element; reactions rearrange atoms into new substances; they happen all around you. Challenge: spot one reaction today — rust, fire, cooking, or fermenting — and name what changed."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Atoms = building blocks · 2) Element = one atom type · 3) Reaction = new substance forms.

## Quiz
1. What is the smallest building block of matter? *(An atom.)* 2. What is an element? *(A substance made of one type of atom.)* 3. What happens in a chemical reaction? *(Atoms rearrange to form a new substance.)*

## Teacher (45 min)
"What is everything made of?" → teach atoms and elements → molecule example (water) → demo a safe reaction (rust photos, fizzing) → spot-a-reaction homework.
