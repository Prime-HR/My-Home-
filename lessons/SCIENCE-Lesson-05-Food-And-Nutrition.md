# Pillar 1 · Integrated Science — Lesson 5: Food & Nutrition Science
### "Why some children grow strong on simple food and others fall sick on plenty"

> Paste into **NotebookLM** to generate slides/audio. **Say it** = narration/teleprompter.

**Subject:** 1.2 Integrated Science · **Lesson:** 5 · **Audience:** Everyone 🇬🇭 + JHS · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Name the food groups. 2) Build a balanced plate from local foods. 3) Understand deficiency diseases.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** Eating plenty is not the same as eating WELL. 🍲
**Say it:** "A child can eat a full bowl every day and still grow weak, while another stays strong on simple food. The difference isn't quantity — it's nutrition science. Let's learn what your body actually needs."

## SLIDE 2 — The job of food
**On-screen:** Energy · build & repair · protect. 🔋
**Say it:** "Food does three jobs: gives energy, builds and repairs your body, and protects you from sickness. A good meal does all three."

## SLIDE 3 — The food groups (local)
**On-screen:** Carbs · Proteins · Vitamins/Minerals · Fats · Water. 🥗
**Say it:** "Carbohydrates for energy — banku, yam, rice. Proteins to build — beans, fish, eggs, groundnuts. Vitamins and minerals to protect — kontomire, garden eggs, fruits. Plus healthy fats and plenty of water."
**🎓 Go deeper:** A meal with all groups is a **balanced diet**."

## SLIDE 4 — When something is missing
**On-screen:** Too little protein → kwashiorkor. ⚠️
**Say it:** "Missing nutrients cause real diseases. Too little protein causes kwashiorkor; too little iron causes anaemia and tiredness; too little vitamin A harms the eyes. These are preventable with the right local foods."

## SLIDE 5 — The hidden dangers
**On-screen:** Too much sugar, oil, soft drinks. 🥤
**Say it:** "The other side: too much sugar, oil, and soft drinks leads to overweight, diabetes, and heart problems — now rising fast in Ghana. Balance, not excess, is health."

## SLIDE 6 — Eat smart on a budget
**On-screen:** Cheap local foods can be the healthiest. 💰
**Say it:** "You don't need expensive imported food. Beans, kontomire, groundnuts, and fruits in season are cheap and powerful. Smart eating is about wisdom, not money."

## SLIDE 7 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Eat to build, not just to fill. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: food gives energy, builds, and protects; balance all the groups; missing nutrients cause disease; avoid too much sugar and oil. Challenge: plan one balanced meal today using only local foods."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Energy, build, protect · 2) Balance the groups · 3) Local can be the healthiest.

## Quiz
1. Name the 3 jobs of food. *(Energy, build/repair, protect.)* 2. Which nutrient builds the body? *(Protein.)* 3. What causes anaemia? *(Too little iron.)*

## Teacher (45 min)
"What did you eat today?" → sort into groups → teach balanced diet → discuss deficiency diseases → plan a balanced local meal.
