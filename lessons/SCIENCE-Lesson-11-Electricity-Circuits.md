# Pillar 1 · Integrated Science — Lesson 11: Electricity & Circuits
### "The invisible worker that lights your home — and how to respect it"

**Subject:** 1.2 Integrated Science · **Lesson:** 11 · **Audience:** Everyone 🇬🇭 + JHS · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Understand what electric current is. 2) Know what a circuit needs. 3) Stay safe with electricity.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** Flip a switch and light appears. But do you know what's flowing? 💡
**Say it:** "We flip switches all day without a thought. But electricity is a powerful, invisible worker — and one that can also kill if disrespected. Understanding it makes you safer and smarter."

## SLIDE 2 — What is electricity?
**On-screen:** A flow of tiny charges through wires. ⚡
**Say it:** "Electricity is a flow of tiny electric charges moving through a wire — like water flowing through a pipe. We call that flow current, and it carries energy to do work."

## SLIDE 3 — A circuit
**On-screen:** Source + wires + a complete loop + a switch. 🔄
**Say it:** "For electricity to flow, it needs a complete loop called a circuit: a source (battery or mains), wires, the device, and a switch to open or close the path. Break the loop and the flow stops — that's what a switch does."
**🎓 Go deeper:** Materials that let current flow are **conductors** (metals); those that block it are **insulators** (rubber, plastic)."

## SLIDE 4 — Electricity does work
**On-screen:** Light, heat, motion, sound. 🔦
**Say it:** "Current turns into useful work — light in a bulb, heat in an iron, motion in a fan, sound in a speaker. Same electricity, many jobs, depending on the device."

## SLIDE 5 — Respect it: safety
**On-screen:** Dry hands · no overloading · cover sockets. ⚠️
**Say it:** "Electricity demands respect. Never touch switches with wet hands, don't overload sockets, keep children away from bare wires, and report exposed cables. These rules prevent shocks and fires."

## SLIDE 6 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Understand it, use it, respect it. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: electricity is flowing charge; it needs a complete circuit; it gives light, heat, motion, and sound; and it must be handled safely. Challenge: trace one circuit in your home — from switch to device — and check for any unsafe wiring."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Current = flowing charge · 2) Needs a complete circuit · 3) Respect safety rules.

## Quiz
