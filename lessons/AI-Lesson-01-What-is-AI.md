# AI For Ghana — Lesson 1: What is AI?

**Lesson:** 1 of 10 (Phase 1 — Foundation: What is AI?)
**Topic:** What is Artificial Intelligence?
**Big analogy:** A Kejetia market woman who has sold for 20 years
**Audience:** Everyone 🇬🇭 (market traders, drivers, parents, youth) + JHS/SHS students
**Duration:** 30-sec TikTok cut · 45-min class version
**By Rauf Husein · AI For Ghana · raufhusein.site**

**By the end, you will be able to:**
1. Say what AI is in one simple sentence — in your own words.
2. Point to 3 places AI is already in your daily Ghanaian life.
3. Explain the one secret word that makes AI work: **data**.

---

## SLIDE 1 — The hook
**On-screen:** You use AI every day… and you don't even know it. 🤔🇬🇭

**Say it:** "Before you scroll — answer me one thing. You used artificial intelligence today already. On your own phone. You didn't notice. By the end of this lesson, you'll see it everywhere."

**🇬🇭 Why it stops you:** It's not "computer talk." It's about the phone in your hand right now.

---

## SLIDE 2 — The whole lesson in one line
**On-screen:** AI = a machine that **learns from experience.**

**Say it:** "Forget the big grammar. Here is all of it in one line: AI is a machine that learns from experience. That's it. Now let me show you with someone you already know and respect."

**🎓 Go deeper (students):** The proper name for "learning from experience" is **machine learning** — the engine inside most modern AI.

---

## SLIDE 3 — Meet the teacher: the Kejetia market woman
**On-screen:** She has sold at Kejetia for **20 years.** She is a genius. 👩🏾‍🦱🍅

**Say it:** "Picture the market woman at Kejetia in Kumasi. Twenty years selling tomatoes. She knows every price. She knows what sells in the morning and what sells in the evening. She can serve three customers and still know who is trying to cheat her. Nobody gave her a textbook. She learned it all from experience — from doing it, day after day."

**🇬🇭 Ghana truth:** This is real intelligence. We just don't usually call it that.

---

## SLIDE 4 — Now meet AI
**On-screen:** AI is like her — but it has seen **a BILLION transactions.** 🤯

**Say it:** "Now imagine that same market woman — but instead of 20 years, she has seen one billion sales. She never forgets a single one. She never sleeps. She never gets tired. And every day, she gets a little bit smarter. That is artificial intelligence. Same idea as our market woman — just at a speed no human can reach."

**🎓 Go deeper (students):** The "billion transactions" the AI studies is called **training data**. More good data → a smarter AI.

---

## SLIDE 5 — The one secret word
**On-screen:** Experience has a name: **DATA.** 📊

**Say it:** "Here is the secret. For the market woman, 'experience' means years of selling. For AI, that experience is called data — pictures, words, numbers, prices, voices. Feed it good data and it learns. Feed it rubbish and it learns rubbish. Same as a child: what you teach is what they know."

**🎓 Go deeper (students):** Remember three words — **Data** (the experience), **Algorithm** (the recipe for learning), **Model** (the trained 'brain' you end up with).

---

## SLIDE 6 — It's already in your pocket
**On-screen:** You already use AI every single day 👇

**Say it:** "Don't believe AI is far away in America. Look at your own phone today."

- 📍 **Google Maps** finds the fastest route past Accra traffic — that's AI.
- 💬 **WhatsApp** finishes your word before you type it — that's AI.
- 🔒 **Face unlock** knows it's you and not your brother — that's AI.
- 📱 **TikTok "For You"** keeps showing what you like — that's AI.
- 💰 **MoMo / your bank** flags a strange transaction as fraud — that's AI.

**🇬🇭 Why it matters:** You are already an AI user. Now you'll become someone who *understands* it.

---

## SLIDE 7 — What AI is NOT
**On-screen:** AI is clever — but it is **not magic, and not always right.** ⚠️

**Say it:** "Be wise. AI is not a human being. It has no heart and no common sense of its own. And it can be wrong — confidently wrong — like a hot rumour that flies round the market but turns out to be false. So we use it, but we still think for ourselves and we check important things."

**🎓 Go deeper (students):** When AI makes up something false but says it like it's true, that's called a **hallucination**. Always verify.

---

## SLIDE 8 — Why this matters for Ghana (2025 → 2050)
**On-screen:** The ones who understand AI will lead. Why not us? 🇬🇭🚀

**Say it:** "By 2050, Africa will have the largest young workforce on earth, and most good jobs will touch AI. The farmer who uses AI to know when to plant. The trader who uses it to write her adverts. The student who uses it to prepare for WASSCE. This is not about replacing the market woman's wisdom — it's about giving every Ghanaian that same superpower."

**🇬🇭 The mission:** Make Ghana the AI capital of West Africa by 2030.

---

## SLIDE 9 — Recap + your challenge
**On-screen:** You now know what AI is. ✅ Teach one person today.

**Say it:** "Quick recap: AI is a machine that learns from experience. Its experience is called data. And it's already in your maps, your keyboard, your MoMo. Your challenge: explain AI to one person today using your OWN Ghanaian example. That's how we move a nation."

**Recap in 3:**
1. AI = a machine that **learns from experience.**
2. Experience = **data.**
3. It's already in your **phone, MoMo, and maps.**

## Teacher version (45-minute class)
- **0–5 min — Starter:** Play the 30-sec market woman video. Ask: "Who here is intelligent without a textbook?"
- **5–15 min — Instruction:** Walk Slides 2–5. Land the line "AI learns from experience = data."
- **15–25 min — Activity:** In groups, list 5 places students have seen AI on their phones (Slide 6).
- **25–35 min — Create:** Each group invents ONE new Ghanaian analogy for AI (e.g., "AI is like a trotro mate who never forgets a fare").
- **35–45 min — Share & assess:** Groups present. Success = student explains AI in their own words with a local example.
