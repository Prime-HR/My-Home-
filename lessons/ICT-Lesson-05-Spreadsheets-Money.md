## SLIDE 1 — The hook
**On-screen:** Most businesses don't fail from no sales — but from not counting. 📉
**Say it:** "Here's a hard truth. Many Ghanaian businesses don't collapse because they have no customers — they collapse because the owner never knew their real profit. A free tool fixes that today: the spreadsheet."

## SLIDE 2 — What is a spreadsheet?
**On-screen:** A grid of boxes for numbers — that does the maths for you. 🔢
**Say it:** "A spreadsheet is a grid of little boxes called cells. You type your numbers in, and it can add, subtract, and calculate automatically. The free one is Google Sheets; Microsoft Excel is the other big name."

## SLIDE 3 — Money in, money out
**On-screen:** Two columns: what you EARN, what you SPEND. 💰
**Say it:** "Start simple. One column for money coming IN (sales). One column for money going OUT (costs — goods, transport, data). When you can see both, you finally see the truth of your business."

## SLIDE 4 — The magic of formulas
**On-screen:** =SUM() adds your numbers for you. ➕
**Say it:** "Here's the magic. Instead of counting with your head, type '=SUM(' and select your sales — it totals them instantly. Change one number and the total updates by itself. The computer never makes an addition mistake."
**🎓 Go deeper (students):** A **formula** starts with '=' . SUM adds; you can also subtract to find profit: income − expenses.

## SLIDE 5 — Find your real profit
**On-screen:** Profit = Money In − Money Out. 🎯
**Say it:** "Now the most important number in any business: profit. Total in, minus total out. If it's positive, you're winning. If it's negative, you're paying to work — and now you KNOW, so you can fix it."

## SLIDE 6 — Beyond business
**On-screen:** Budget your salary, school fees, savings. 🏠
**Say it:** "This isn't only for traders. Track your salary and spending, plan school fees, set a savings goal. People who write their money down keep more of it. It's the quiet habit of every person who builds wealth."

## SLIDE 7 — Let AI build it for you
**On-screen:** Ask AI: "Make me a simple sales tracker in Sheets." 🤖
**Say it:** "Don't know where to start? Ask an AI to design a simple tracker and even tell you the formulas to type. Technology removing every excuse — that's the goal."

## SLIDE 8 — Why it matters
**On-screen:** Numbers you can see are numbers you can grow. 📈
**Say it:** "What you measure, you can improve. A trader who tracks for one month suddenly sees where money leaks — and plugs it. That's how a small hustle becomes a real business."

## SLIDE 9 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Count, and you'll keep more. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: a spreadsheet is a grid that does your maths; track money in and out; use =SUM to total; profit is in minus out. Challenge: open Google Sheets and track your money for the next 7 days."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Spreadsheet = grid that calculates · 2) Track in & out · 3) Profit = in − out.

## Teacher version (45 min)
- 0–5: "Who knows their exact profit/savings this month?" Few will.
- 5–18: Build a 2-column tracker live; introduce =SUM.
- 18–35: Students build their own tracker with sample data.
- 35–42: Calculate profit; discuss what the numbers reveal.
- 42–45: Homework — track real money for a week.
