# AI For Ghana — Lesson 6: AI for Farmers
### "The farmer with a smartphone is becoming the richest farmer"

## SLIDE 1 — The hook
**On-screen:** Two farmers, same land. One has AI. Guess who wins. 🌾📱
**Say it:** "Two farmers plant the same crop on the same soil. One guesses; the other uses AI to know exactly when to plant, what's wrong with a sick leaf, and where to sell for the best price. Same land — very different harvest."

## SLIDE 2 — One line
**On-screen:** AI turns farming from guessing into knowing. 🎯
**Say it:** "Farming has always involved guessing the weather, the pests, the prices. AI replaces guessing with knowing."

## SLIDE 3 — Spot disease from a photo
**On-screen:** Snap a sick leaf → AI names the disease. 📸
**Say it:** "Take a photo of a sick cassava or cocoa leaf, and AI apps can identify the disease and suggest treatment — in seconds. No waiting weeks for an expert to visit."
**🎓 Go deeper:** This is **image recognition** — the same AI skill as face unlock, used for crops.

## SLIDE 4 — Know when to plant
**On-screen:** AI reads the weather so you plant at the right time. 🌧️
**Say it:** "AI studies weather patterns and advises the best planting and harvesting windows. In a changing climate with unpredictable rains, this can save a whole season."

## SLIDE 5 — Sell at the best price
**On-screen:** Check real market prices before you sell. 💰
**Say it:** "AI-powered platforms show real market prices across regions, so a farmer in Techiman isn't cheated by a middleman. Knowledge is bargaining power."

## SLIDE 6 — Ghana is already doing it
**On-screen:** Ghanaian agri-tech is leading. 🇬🇭
**Say it:** "This isn't a foreign dream. Ghanaian companies like Farmerline and others already put market info, weather, and advice into farmers' phones — even by voice, in local languages. We are building it here."

## SLIDE 7 — Farming is now a tech career
**On-screen:** Smart farming attracts smart youth. 🚜
**Say it:** "To the young person who thinks farming is 'old work' — think again. Modern farming uses drones, sensors, data, and AI. It's one of the biggest business opportunities of your lifetime, and Africa will feed the world."

## SLIDE 8 — Why it matters
**On-screen:** More food, more income, more pride. 🌍
**Say it:** "Better harvests mean more food security, higher incomes, and stronger communities. AI in farming isn't about machines replacing farmers — it's about farmers becoming unstoppable."

## SLIDE 9 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Smart farming wins. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: AI helps farmers spot disease, time planting, and sell at the best price — and Ghana is already building it. Challenge: find one farming app or AI tool and show it to a farmer you know."
**Recap in 3:** 1) AI = knowing, not guessing · 2) Disease, weather, prices · 3) Farming is a tech career.

## Quick Quiz
1. Name 2 ways AI helps farmers. *(Disease detection, planting timing, market prices.)*
2. What AI skill identifies a disease from a leaf photo? *(Image recognition.)*
3. True or false: farming has no future for youth. *(False — it's a major tech career.)*

## Teacher version (45 min)
- 0–5: "Who has a farmer in the family?" Discuss their biggest challenges.
- 5–18: Map each challenge to an AI solution.
- 18–30: Activity — design a simple "smart farm" on paper using AI tools.
- 30–40: Discuss Ghana agri-tech examples.
- 40–45: Each writes one AI idea to share with a farmer.
