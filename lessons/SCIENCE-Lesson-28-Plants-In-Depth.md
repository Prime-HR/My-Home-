# Pillar 1 · Integrated Science — Lesson 28: Plants in Depth
### "How a tiny seed becomes a cocoa tree — the science behind Ghana's farms"

**Subject:** 1.2 Integrated Science · **Lesson:** 28 · **Audience:** JHS/SHS 🇬🇭 + farmers · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Name the parts of a plant and their jobs. 2) Understand how plants drink and reproduce. 3) Apply it to better farming.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** A seed smaller than your fingernail becomes a tree feeding a nation. 🌱➡️🌳
**Say it:** "Ghana's wealth — cocoa, maize, plantain — all begins as a tiny seed. Understanding how plants work makes better farmers and protects the crops that feed and earn for our nation. Let's grow our knowledge."

## SLIDE 2 — Parts of a plant
**On-screen:** Roots · stem · leaves · flowers. 🌿
**Say it:** "Each part has a job. ROOTS anchor the plant and drink water and minerals. The STEM holds it up and carries water around. LEAVES make food by photosynthesis. FLOWERS make seeds for the next generation."

## SLIDE 3 — How plants drink
**On-screen:** Roots → stem → leaves (transport). 💧
**Say it:** "Water rises from the roots, up through the stem, to the leaves, where some is used and the rest escapes as vapour. This flow carries minerals from the soil to the whole plant — which is why healthy soil matters."
**🎓 Go deeper:** Water loss from leaves is **transpiration**; it pulls more water up."

## SLIDE 4 — Pollination & seeds
**On-screen:** Bees and wind move pollen → fruit & seeds. 🐝
**Say it:** "Flowers make seeds only after pollination — pollen carried by insects like bees, or by wind, from one flower to another. This is why bees are precious: no pollination, no cocoa, no fruits, no harvest."

## SLIDE 5 — What plants need to thrive
**On-screen:** Sunlight · water · healthy soil · space. ☀️
**Say it:** "Strong plants need sunlight, enough water, healthy soil with minerals, and space to grow. Farmers who understand this — spacing crops, feeding soil, protecting pollinators — get bigger, better harvests."

## SLIDE 6 — Better farming for Ghana
**On-screen:** Science = bigger harvests, less waste. 🚜
**Say it:** "This science is money for Ghana. Knowing a plant's needs means smarter planting, healthier soil, protected bees, and stronger yields. Modern, science-based farming is one of our biggest opportunities."

## SLIDE 7 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Know the plant, grow the harvest. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: roots drink, stem carries, leaves feed, flowers seed; water flows up and escapes as vapour; pollination by bees and wind makes seeds; plants need sun, water, soil, space. Challenge: look at a real plant and name each part's job."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Roots/stem/leaves/flowers · 2) Water rises; pollination makes seeds · 3) Care for soil & bees.
