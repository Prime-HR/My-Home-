# Pillar 1 · Integrated Science — Lesson 30: Mixtures, Solutions & Separation
### "How salt is won from the sea and clean water from dirty — the science of separating"

**Subject:** 1.2 Integrated Science · **Lesson:** 30 · **Audience:** JHS/SHS 🇬🇭 · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Tell a mixture from a solution. 2) Name ways to separate mixtures. 3) Link it to local industry.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** How do they get pure salt from the sea at Ada? Science. 🧂
**Say it:** "At Ada, people harvest salt straight from sea water. They turn dirty water clean and separate gold from rock. Behind it all is the science of mixtures and how to separate them. Let's learn these powerful, practical skills."

## SLIDE 2 — Mixtures
**On-screen:** Two things mixed but not chemically joined. 🥗
**Say it:** "A mixture is two or more things mixed together without joining chemically — like rice and beans, or sand and water. Because they're not bonded, we can separate them again."

## SLIDE 3 — Solutions
**On-screen:** Something dissolved evenly (salt in water). 💧
**Say it:** "A solution is a special mixture where one thing dissolves completely and evenly into another — salt or sugar into water. It looks like one liquid, but the salt is still there, hidden among the water particles."
**🎓 Go deeper:** The thing that dissolves is the **solute**; what it dissolves in is the **solvent**."

## SLIDE 4 — Separation methods
**On-screen:** Filter · evaporate · distil · sieve. ⚗️
**Say it:** "We separate mixtures cleverly. FILTERING removes solids from liquids — like sieving. EVAPORATION boils off water to leave salt behind — that's how sea salt is made. DISTILLATION evaporates then re-collects pure water — how dirty water becomes clean."

## SLIDE 5 — Real Ghana uses
**On-screen:** Salt, clean water, gold, gari, oil. 🏭
**Say it:** "These methods run real industries: salt from the sea, clean drinking water, separating gold, pressing palm oil, processing gari. Separation science is everyday Ghanaian work and business."

## SLIDE 6 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Mix it, then master separating it. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: a mixture is unbonded substances; a solution is one dissolved into another; we separate by filtering, evaporating, distilling, sieving; these run real industries. Challenge: separate a simple mixture at home — like sand and water by filtering."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Mixture vs solution · 2) Filter/evaporate/distil/sieve · 3) Powers local industry.
