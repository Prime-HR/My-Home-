# Pillar 1 · Integrated Science — Lesson 45: Diet-Related Diseases
### "The new killers in Ghana aren't germs — they're on our plates"

> Paste into **NotebookLM** to generate slides/audio. **Say it** = narration/teleprompter.

**Subject:** 1.2 Integrated Science · **Lesson:** 45 · **Audience:** Everyone 🇬🇭 + JHS/SHS · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Understand lifestyle diseases. 2) Know the main risks. 3) Make protective food choices.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** More Ghanaians now suffer from sugar and salt than from hunger. 🍔
**Say it:** "For generations our enemy was too little food. Today a new enemy rises — too much sugar, salt, and oil. Diabetes and high blood pressure are spreading fast. The good news: food science can protect you."

## SLIDE 2 — Lifestyle diseases
**On-screen:** Caused over time by how we live and eat. ⏳
**Say it:** "These are diseases that build up slowly from daily habits — what we eat, how little we move. They include diabetes, high blood pressure, and heart disease. Unlike malaria, they creep in silently."

## SLIDE 3 — Diabetes
**On-screen:** Too much sugar overwhelms the body. 🩸
**Say it:** "Diabetes is when the body can't control its blood sugar, often from too much sugary food and being overweight. It damages eyes, kidneys, and nerves over time — but it's largely preventable."
**🎓 Go deeper:** The body controls sugar with a hormone called **insulin**."

## SLIDE 4 — High blood pressure
**On-screen:** Too much salt strains the heart. 🧂
**Say it:** "High blood pressure, often from too much salt, stress, and weight, silently strains the heart and can cause stroke. It's called the 'silent killer' because you may feel fine until it's serious."

## SLIDE 5 — Protect yourself
**On-screen:** Less sugar/salt/oil · more veg · move daily. 🥗
**Say it:** "The defence is simple and cheap: eat less sugar, salt, and fried food; eat more vegetables, beans, and fruit; drink water not soft drinks; and move your body daily. Prevention beats any medicine."

## SLIDE 6 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Your fork is a tool for health or harm. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: lifestyle diseases build from daily habits; diabetes comes from too much sugar, high blood pressure from too much salt; both are largely preventable with good food and movement. Challenge: cut one sugary or salty thing from your day this week."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Lifestyle diseases build slowly · 2) Sugar→diabetes, salt→blood pressure · 3) Prevent with food + movement.

## Quiz
1. Name two diet-related diseases. *(Diabetes, high blood pressure, heart disease.)* 2. What hormone controls blood sugar? *(Insulin.)* 3. Name one way to prevent these diseases. *(Less sugar/salt; more veg; exercise.)*
