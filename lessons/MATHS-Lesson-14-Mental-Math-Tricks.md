## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** The trader who calculates fast NEVER gets cheated. ⚡
**Say it:** "At the market, the slow counter loses. The fast one looks sharp, gives correct change, and earns trust. Mental math isn't magic — it's a few tricks anyone can learn. Let's make you quick."

## SLIDE 2 — Round, then adjust
**On-screen:** 49 + 28 → 50 + 28 − 1 = 77. 🔄
**Say it:** "Hard numbers become easy when you round. 49 plus 28? Make it 50 plus 28 is 78, then take away the 1 you added — 77. Round to friendly numbers, then fix."

## SLIDE 3 — 10% is your best friend
**On-screen:** 10% = move the dot one place. ➡️
**Say it:** "To find 10% of any amount, just move the decimal one step left. 10% of 250 is 25. Need 20%? Double it — 50. Need 5%? Halve the 10%. Tips, discounts, and tax made instant."

## SLIDE 4 — Multiply smart
**On-screen:** ×5 = ×10 then halve. ✖️
**Say it:** "To multiply by 5, times by 10 then halve. 14 times 5? 140 halved is 70. To times by 9, times by 10 then subtract the number. Little shortcuts, big speed."

## SLIDE 5 — Estimate to check
**On-screen:** A rough answer catches big mistakes. 🧠
**Say it:** "Always estimate first. If a price should be 'around 200' and the calculator says 2,000, you know something's wrong. Estimation is your error alarm."
**🎓 Go deeper:** Estimating before calculating is what strong mathematicians do automatically."

## SLIDE 6 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Fast maths, sharp mind, safe money. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: round then adjust; 10% is moving the dot; multiply with shortcuts; estimate to check. Challenge: practise giving correct change in your head five times today."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Round & adjust · 2) 10% = move the dot · 3) Estimate to check.
