# Pillar 1 · Integrated Science — Lesson 42: Biodiversity & Conservation
### "Ghana's forests and wildlife are a treasure we are losing fast"

> Paste into **NotebookLM** to generate slides/audio. **Say it** = narration/teleprompter.

**Subject:** 1.2 Integrated Science · **Lesson:** 42 · **Audience:** JHS/SHS 🇬🇭 · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Define biodiversity. 2) Know why it matters. 3) Name ways to conserve it.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** Some animals your grandparents saw, you never will. 🐘
**Say it:** "There are creatures and forests your grandparents knew that are vanishing in your lifetime. This loss is quiet but serious. Let's learn what biodiversity is and why saving it is saving ourselves."

## SLIDE 2 — What is biodiversity?
**On-screen:** The variety of all living things. 🦋
**Say it:** "Biodiversity means the rich variety of life — many kinds of plants, animals, and microbes. Ghana is blessed with it: forests, the Volta, the coast, savannah, all teeming with species."

## SLIDE 3 — Why it matters
**On-screen:** Food, medicine, clean air, balance. 🌿
**Say it:** "Variety keeps nature strong. Different species give us food, medicines (many drugs come from plants), pollination, and resilience. A varied ecosystem survives shocks; a poor one collapses."

## SLIDE 4 — The threats
**On-screen:** Deforestation, galamsey, hunting, pollution. ⚠️
**Say it:** "We're losing biodiversity through cutting forests, galamsey poisoning rivers, over-hunting, and pollution. Each lost species is a page torn from a book we can never rewrite."

## SLIDE 5 — Conservation
**On-screen:** Protect habitats, replant, enforce, educate. 🌳
**Say it:** "We can act: protect forests and reserves, replant trees, stop galamsey and illegal hunting, and educate communities. Ghana's national parks like Kakum and Mole show conservation working."

## SLIDE 6 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Save the variety, save the future. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: biodiversity is the variety of life; it gives food, medicine, and resilience; deforestation and galamsey threaten it; conservation protects it. Challenge: name one Ghanaian animal or tree worth protecting and one way to help."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Biodiversity = variety of life · 2) It feeds & protects us · 3) Conserve habitats.

## Quiz
1. What is biodiversity? *(The variety of all living things.)* 2. Name one benefit of biodiversity. *(Food, medicine, pollination, resilience.)* 3. Name one threat to it. *(Deforestation, galamsey, hunting, pollution.)*
