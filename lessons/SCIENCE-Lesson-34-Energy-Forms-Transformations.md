# Pillar 1 · Integrated Science — Lesson 34: Energy Forms & Transformations
### "Energy is never created or destroyed — only changed from one form to another"

> Paste into **NotebookLM** to generate slides/audio. **Say it** = narration/teleprompter.

**Subject:** 1.2 Integrated Science · **Lesson:** 34 · **Audience:** JHS/SHS 🇬🇭 · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Name forms of energy. 2) Explain energy transformations. 3) Understand the conservation of energy.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** The energy in your food today was once sunlight on a leaf. ☀️➡️💪
**Say it:** "The energy moving your muscles right now travelled from the sun, into a plant, into your food, into you. Energy is always on a journey, changing form but never disappearing. Let's follow it."

## SLIDE 2 — Forms of energy
**On-screen:** Light · heat · sound · movement · electrical · chemical. ⚡
**Say it:** "Energy wears many costumes: light, heat, sound, movement, electrical energy in wires, and chemical energy stored in food and fuel. Same energy, different forms."

## SLIDE 3 — It transforms
**On-screen:** One form changes into another. 🔄
**Say it:** "Energy constantly changes form. A torch turns electrical energy into light. Your body turns chemical energy from food into movement and heat. A speaker turns electrical energy into sound. Life is energy transforming."
**🎓 Go deeper:** A device that changes energy from one form to another is a **transducer** (e.g., a bulb, a speaker)."

## SLIDE 4 — The great law
**On-screen:** Energy is never created or destroyed. ⚖️
**Say it:** "Here's one of science's deepest truths: energy can't be created from nothing or destroyed — only changed from one form to another. The total always stays the same. This is the conservation of energy."

## SLIDE 5 — Wasted energy
**On-screen:** Some always escapes as heat. 🔥
**Say it:** "In every change, some energy escapes as heat we can't use — that's why a phone warms up, a bulb gets hot. Good design wastes less. This is why efficient appliances save you money."

## SLIDE 6 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Follow the energy, save the energy. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: energy comes in many forms; it constantly transforms; it's never created or destroyed; some always escapes as heat. Challenge: trace the energy in one device — like a torch or fan — from source to output."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Many forms of energy · 2) It transforms · 3) Never created/destroyed (some lost as heat).
