# Pillar 1 · Mathematics — Lesson 17: Simple vs Compound Interest
### "The 8th wonder of the world — it can build you or bury you"

> Paste into **NotebookLM** to generate slides/audio. **Say it** = narration/teleprompter.

**Subject:** 1.1 Mathematics · **Lesson:** 17 · **Audience:** Everyone 🇬🇭 + SHS · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Tell simple from compound interest. 2) See how compounding grows. 3) Use it for saving, beware it for debt.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** Save ₵100 a month for 10 years — the interest may shock you. 📈
**Say it:** "There's a force that quietly turns small savings into wealth — and small debts into nightmares. It's called compound interest. Understand it, and time starts working FOR you instead of against you."

## SLIDE 2 — Simple interest
**On-screen:** Interest only on the original amount. ➕
**Say it:** "Simple interest is calculated only on the money you started with. Put 1,000 cedis at 10% simple for 3 years — that's 100 a year, 300 total. Steady and predictable."

## SLIDE 3 — Compound interest
**On-screen:** Interest on your money AND past interest. ❄️➡️⛄
**Say it:** "Compound interest is charged on your money PLUS the interest already earned. Year one earns interest; year two earns interest on the bigger amount; it snowballs, growing faster and faster."
**🎓 Go deeper:** Compounding is exponential — a multiplying pattern from Lesson 13."

## SLIDE 4 — Time is the magic
**On-screen:** The earlier you start, the bigger the snowball. ⏳
**Say it:** "The real power is time. Someone who starts saving at 20 can beat someone who saves more but starts at 40 — because the snowball rolled for longer. Start small, start NOW."

## SLIDE 5 — The dark side: debt
**On-screen:** Compounding debt is how people get trapped. ⚠️
**Say it:** "The same force works against borrowers. Unpaid loan interest compounds, and a small debt balloons. This is why understanding it isn't optional — it's protection."

## SLIDE 6 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Make compounding your servant, not your master. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: simple interest is on the original only; compound is on everything, so it snowballs; time makes it powerful; it builds savers and buries borrowers. Challenge: start a small monthly saving now and imagine it compounding for 10 years."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Simple = original only · 2) Compound = snowballs · 3) Time is the magic.

## Quiz
1. Simple interest is calculated on what? *(The original amount only.)* 2. Why does compound grow faster? *(Interest earns interest.)* 3. What makes compounding powerful? *(Time.)*

## Teacher (45 min)
Compare simple vs compound over 10 years on the board → students see the gap → discuss starting early → debt warning → start-a-saving challenge.
