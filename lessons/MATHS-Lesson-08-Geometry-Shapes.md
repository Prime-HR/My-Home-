# Pillar 1 · Mathematics — Lesson 8: Geometry & Shapes
### "The maths behind every building, every design, and every straight wall"

> Paste into **NotebookLM** to generate slides/audio. **Say it** = narration/teleprompter.

**Subject:** 1.1 Mathematics · **Lesson:** 8 · **Audience:** Everyone 🇬🇭 + JHS/SHS · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Name common shapes and angles. 2) Understand the right angle and why it matters. 3) See geometry in building and design.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** Why doesn't your house lean over? Geometry. 📐🏠
**Say it:** "Look around — every wall, window, roof and tile stays straight and strong because of geometry. The builder who knows it builds well; the one who guesses, builds crooked. Let's see the shapes that hold up your world."

## SLIDE 2 — Shapes everywhere
**On-screen:** Square, rectangle, triangle, circle. 🔷
**Say it:** "Shapes are the alphabet of design. Rectangles for rooms and doors, triangles for strong roofs, circles for wheels and pipes. Each has properties we can measure."

## SLIDE 3 — Angles
**On-screen:** Angle = the turn between two lines, measured in degrees. 📐
**Say it:** "An angle is how much you turn between two lines, measured in degrees. A full turn is 360°, half is 180°, and a quarter — the corner of this page — is 90°."

## SLIDE 4 — The right angle: 90°
**On-screen:** 90° = a perfect corner. Builders live by it. 🧱
**Say it:** "The 90-degree right angle is the most important in building. Walls must meet the floor at 90° or the structure leans and cracks. That's why masons use a set square and a spirit level."
**🎓 Go deeper:** Triangles are the strongest shape — that's why you see them in roofs, bridges, and pylons."

## SLIDE 5 — Geometry in design
**On-screen:** Kente patterns, Adinkra, architecture, phones. 🎨
**Say it:** "Geometry is also beauty. Our Kente and Adinkra are full of repeating shapes and symmetry. Modern design — apps, logos, buildings — is geometry made beautiful."

## SLIDE 6 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Shapes and angles build the world. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: shapes are the building blocks; angles measure turns in degrees; the 90° right angle keeps things straight; triangles are strongest. Challenge: find three right angles and one triangle around you today."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Shapes = design alphabet · 2) Angles in degrees · 3) 90° keeps it straight.

## Quiz
1. How many degrees in a right angle? *(90°.)* 2. Which shape is strongest for roofs? *(Triangle.)* 3. How many degrees in a full turn? *(360°.)*

## Teacher (45 min)
Spot shapes/angles in the classroom → teach angles and the right angle → use a set square → design a pattern with shapes → find-the-angle homework.
