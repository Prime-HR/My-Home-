# Pillar 1 · Mathematics — Lesson 10: Time, Speed & Distance
### "Will the trotro get you there on time? The maths of every journey"

**Subject:** 1.1 Mathematics · **Lesson:** 10 · **Audience:** Everyone 🇬🇭 + JHS/SHS · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Relate speed, distance, and time. 2) Plan journeys and deadlines. 3) Manage time like a resource.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** Accra to Kumasi in how long? Maths answers before you board. 🚌
**Say it:** "Will you reach in time? Should you leave now or in an hour? Every journey and every deadline is a maths question. Master it and you stop being late and stop wasting your day."

## SLIDE 2 — The triangle
**On-screen:** Speed = Distance ÷ Time. ⏱️
**Say it:** "Three friends always travel together: speed, distance, time. Speed is distance divided by time. Cover 100km in 2 hours and your speed is 50km per hour."

## SLIDE 3 — Rearrange for what you need
**On-screen:** Distance = Speed × Time · Time = Distance ÷ Speed. 🔄
**Say it:** "Need distance? Multiply speed by time. Need time? Divide distance by speed. So a 250km trip at 50km/h takes 5 hours. Now you can plan your departure."
**🎓 Go deeper:** Cover the one you want in the triangle (S, D, T) to see the formula."

## SLIDE 4 — Time is money
**On-screen:** Wasted hours never come back. ⏳
**Say it:** "Time is the one resource you can't buy more of. Planning your hours — like the trader who knows the best market times — turns the same day into more income and less stress."

## SLIDE 5 — Plan backwards
**On-screen:** Start from the deadline, work back. ⏰
**Say it:** "Smart people plan backwards. If the exam is at 9 and the journey takes 1 hour, leave by 7:30 to be safe. Working back from the deadline stops last-minute panic."

## SLIDE 6 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Plan the journey, own the day. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: speed is distance over time; rearrange for distance or time; time is precious; plan backwards from deadlines. Challenge: calculate how long one regular trip really takes, and plan your departure."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Speed = Distance ÷ Time · 2) Rearrange as needed · 3) Plan backwards.

## Quiz
1. 120 km in 2 hours — what speed? *(60 km/h.)* 2. At 40 km/h, how far in 3 hours? *(120 km.)* 3. Distance 100km, speed 50km/h — time? *(2 hours.)*
