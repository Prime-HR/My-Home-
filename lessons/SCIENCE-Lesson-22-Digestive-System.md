# Pillar 1 · Integrated Science — Lesson 22: The Digestive System
### "The amazing journey of banku — from your mouth to your muscles"

> Paste into **NotebookLM** to generate slides/audio. **Say it** = narration/teleprompter.

**Subject:** 1.2 Integrated Science · **Lesson:** 22 · **Audience:** JHS/SHS 🇬🇭 + adults · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Trace the path of food through the body. 2) Explain how food becomes energy. 3) Protect your gut health.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** Your banku takes a 9-metre journey to power your body. 🍲
**Say it:** "When you swallow banku and soup, it begins an incredible journey through about 9 metres of tubing inside you, being broken down into the energy that moves your muscles. Let's follow the food."

## SLIDE 2 — It starts in the mouth
**On-screen:** Chewing + saliva begin digestion. 👄
**Say it:** "Digestion starts the moment you chew. Teeth break food small, and saliva starts breaking it down chemically. That's why chewing well helps your stomach — it's already half done."

## SLIDE 3 — The stomach
**On-screen:** Acid and muscles mash it into a paste. 🌀
**Say it:** "Food slides down to the stomach, where strong acid and churning muscles turn it into a paste and kill many germs. Remember the acids lesson — your stomach is doing real chemistry."

## SLIDE 4 — The small intestine
**On-screen:** Nutrients pass into the blood here. 🩸
**Say it:** "In the long small intestine, the useful nutrients — energy, proteins, vitamins — are absorbed into your blood and carried to every cell. This is where food truly becomes YOU."
**🎓 Go deeper:** Breaking food into absorbable pieces is helped by **enzymes**."

## SLIDE 5 — The large intestine & waste
**On-screen:** Water is reclaimed; waste leaves. 🚽
**Say it:** "What's left moves to the large intestine, where water is absorbed back, and the solid waste is removed from the body. Drinking enough water keeps this smooth and prevents constipation."

## SLIDE 6 — Protect your gut
**On-screen:** Chew well · clean food/water · fibre · don't overeat. 🥗
**Say it:** "Care for this system: eat clean food and water to avoid germs, include fibre from vegetables and fruit, chew properly, and don't overload it. A healthy gut means a healthy you."

## SLIDE 7 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Know the journey of your food. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: digestion starts in the mouth, the stomach uses acid, the small intestine absorbs nutrients to the blood, the large intestine removes waste. Challenge: name each stage as you eat your next meal."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Mouth → stomach → small → large intestine · 2) Small intestine absorbs nutrients · 3) Clean food + fibre + water.
