## SLIDE 1 — The hook
**On-screen:** One typing skill can write your essay AND win you a job. ⌨️💼
**Say it:** "The skill that types your school essay is the very same skill that creates a CV that gets you hired, a business proposal that wins a contract, and a letter that opens doors. Most people type badly. Let's make you the one who types like a professional."

## SLIDE 2 — What is word processing?
**On-screen:** Creating documents on a computer or phone. 📄
**Say it:** "Word processing simply means writing and formatting documents digitally — letters, CVs, reports. The popular tools are Microsoft Word and Google Docs, both of which work on phone and computer. Google Docs is free."

## SLIDE 3 — Format like a pro
**On-screen:** Headings · Bold · Bullets · Spacing. ✨
**Say it:** "Four tools turn messy text into a clean document. Use HEADINGS to organise. Use BOLD for important words. Use BULLET POINTS for lists. And leave SPACING so it breathes. A neat document earns instant respect."

## SLIDE 4 — The golden rules
**On-screen:** One clean font · don't SHOUT · proofread. 🧹
**Say it:** "Three rules. Pick one simple font and stick to it. Don't write everything in CAPITALS — it looks like shouting. And always read it twice for mistakes; one typo can cost you a job."

## SLIDE 5 — Your CV: what goes in
**On-screen:** Name · Contact · About · Education · Skills · Experience. 📋
**Say it:** "A CV is your one-page advert for yourself. Top: your name and contact. Then a short 'about me'. Then education, your skills, and any work or attachment experience. Keep it to one page if you can."

## SLIDE 6 — Make it stand out (cleanly)
**On-screen:** Clear, honest, tailored. No clutter. 🎯
**Say it:** "Don't decorate it like a party invitation. Employers want clear and honest. Match your skills to the job you want. And never lie — it always catches up with you."

## SLIDE 7 — Let AI help (then check)
**On-screen:** Ask AI to improve wording — you keep the truth. 🤖
**Say it:** "Here's a modern advantage: write your CV, then ask an AI like ChatGPT to improve the wording and fix grammar. It makes you sound polished. But keep every fact true and in your own voice."

## SLIDE 8 — Why it matters
**On-screen:** A good document opens doors a good person deserves. 🚪
**Say it:** "Talented people miss opportunities because their CV looked careless. Don't let that be you. This one skill quietly decides who gets the interview."

## SLIDE 9 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Build your CV today. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: word processing creates clean documents; use headings, bold, bullets, spacing; keep your CV one page, clear and honest; let AI polish the words. Challenge: open Google Docs and start your CV today."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Format = headings, bold, bullets, spacing · 2) CV = 1-page honest advert · 3) Let AI polish, keep it true.

## Quick Quiz
1. Name 2 word-processing tools. *(Microsoft Word, Google Docs.)*
2. Name 3 things a CV must include. *(Name/contact, about, education, skills, experience.)*
3. Why not type in all CAPITALS? *(It reads as shouting / unprofessional.)*

## Teacher version (45 min)
- 0–5: Show a messy vs neat document — which gets the job?
- 5–18: Teach formatting tools live (headings, bold, bullets).
- 18–35: Students build their own one-page CV.
- 35–42: Peer review for clarity and typos.
- 42–45: Save and keep — a real CV they own.
