# Pillar 1 · Integrated Science — Lesson 27: Microorganisms — Friend & Foe
### "The tiny creatures that ferment your banku and bake your bread — and some that harm"

> Paste into **NotebookLM** to generate slides/audio. **Say it** = narration/teleprompter.

**Subject:** 1.2 Integrated Science · **Lesson:** 27 · **Audience:** JHS/SHS 🇬🇭 + adults · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Know that microbes can help or harm. 2) Understand fermentation. 3) Understand antibiotics and resistance.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** The same tiny creatures that ferment your banku can also make you sick. 🦠
**Say it:** "We met germs as enemies. But here's the twist: many tiny living things are our friends — they ferment banku and pito, raise our bread, even live helpfully in our gut. Let's meet both sides of the microbe."

## SLIDE 2 — What are microorganisms?
**On-screen:** Living things too small to see. 🔬
**Say it:** "Microorganisms — bacteria, yeasts, fungi, and viruses — are living things too small for the eye. They are everywhere: in soil, water, air, food, and inside you."

## SLIDE 3 — The friendly microbes
**On-screen:** Fermentation: banku, pito, bread, yoghurt. 🍞
**Say it:** "Friendly microbes work for us. Yeast ferments to raise bread and brew pito; bacteria sour and preserve foods like banku and yoghurt. Without these tiny helpers, much of our food wouldn't exist."
**🎓 Go deeper:** Fermentation is microbes turning sugars into useful products — an old, vital chemistry."

## SLIDE 4 — Helpers inside you
**On-screen:** Good gut bacteria aid digestion. 🦠➕
**Say it:** "Your gut holds trillions of helpful bacteria that aid digestion and protect against bad ones. Eating well feeds your good microbes — they're part of your health team."

## SLIDE 5 — The harmful ones
**On-screen:** Some cause disease and spoil food. ⚠️
**Say it:** "Other microbes harm us — causing infections and spoiling food. That's why we cook, refrigerate, and keep clean: to stop harmful microbes from multiplying."

## SLIDE 6 — Antibiotics & resistance
**On-screen:** Use antibiotics correctly — or they stop working. 💊
**Say it:** "Antibiotics are medicines that kill harmful bacteria — a great gift. But misusing them (not finishing the dose, taking them for everything) lets bacteria become resistant and survive. Use them only as a doctor directs. This is a serious global issue."

## SLIDE 7 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Befriend the good, block the bad. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: microbes are tiny living things, some helpful, some harmful; fermentation makes our foods; good gut bacteria aid health; cleanliness stops the bad; use antibiotics correctly. Challenge: name one fermented Ghanaian food and the microbe behind it."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Microbes help & harm · 2) Fermentation makes food · 3) Use antibiotics correctly.

## Quiz
1. Name a food made by friendly microbes. *(Banku, pito, bread, yoghurt.)* 2. What is fermentation? *(Microbes turning sugars into useful products.)* 3. Why finish a full antibiotic course? *(To prevent resistance/surviving bacteria.)*
