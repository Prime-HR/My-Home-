# Pillar 1 · Integrated Science — Lesson 23: The Circulatory System
### "Your heart beats 100,000 times a day and never asks for a break"

> Paste into **NotebookLM** to generate slides/audio. **Say it** = narration/teleprompter.

**Subject:** 1.2 Integrated Science · **Lesson:** 23 · **Audience:** JHS/SHS 🇬🇭 + adults · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Explain what blood and the heart do. 2) Understand blood groups. 3) Protect your heart.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** A fist-sized pump moves your blood 100,000 times today. ❤️
**Say it:** "Right now, a muscle the size of your fist is pumping blood to every corner of your body — about 100,000 beats a day, your whole life, without rest. Let's meet your hardest worker: the heart."

## SLIDE 2 — Blood: the delivery system
**On-screen:** Carries oxygen, food, and fighters. 🩸
**Say it:** "Blood is your body's delivery and defence system. It carries oxygen from the lungs and food from digestion to every cell, takes away waste, and carries the white cells that fight germs."

## SLIDE 3 — The heart: the pump
**On-screen:** It pushes blood through the whole body. 🫀
**Say it:** "The heart is a powerful muscle pump. It pushes blood out to the body and lungs and receives it back, over and over. When you exercise and feel it beat faster, it's working to deliver more oxygen."

## SLIDE 4 — The roads: blood vessels
**On-screen:** Arteries out, veins back, capillaries deliver. 🛣️
**Say it:** "Blood travels through vessels — arteries carry it away from the heart, veins bring it back, and tiny capillaries reach every cell. It's a delivery network longer than you can imagine."
**🎓 Go deeper:** Arteries carry oxygen-rich blood from the heart; veins return it."

## SLIDE 5 — Blood groups
**On-screen:** A, B, AB, O — they must match. 🅰️🅱️
**Say it:** "People have different blood groups — A, B, AB, and O. In a transfusion, the groups must match or the body rejects it. Knowing your blood group can one day save your life — or let you save another's by donating."

## SLIDE 6 — Protect your heart
**On-screen:** Exercise · good food · no smoking · less salt. 🏃🏾
**Say it:** "Heart disease is rising in Ghana. Protect your pump: move daily, eat less salt and fried food, don't smoke, and manage stress. A strong heart is a long life."

## SLIDE 7 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Honour the heart that never rests. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: blood delivers oxygen, food, and defenders; the heart pumps it through arteries, veins, and capillaries; blood groups must match; protect your heart with exercise and good food. Challenge: find out your blood group, and feel your pulse after a short run."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Blood delivers & defends · 2) Heart pumps through vessels · 3) Blood groups must match.

## Quiz
1. What does blood carry? *(Oxygen, food, waste, germ-fighters.)* 2. Arteries carry blood which way? *(Away from the heart.)* 3. Name the 4 blood groups. *(A, B, AB, O.)*
