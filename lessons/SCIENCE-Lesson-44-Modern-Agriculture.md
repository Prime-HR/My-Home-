## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** Africa will feed the world by 2050 — if it farms smart. 🌍🌾
**Say it:** "Our population is growing fast, and Africa holds much of the world's farmland. The future belongs to farmers who use science. Let's see how modern agriculture turns sweat into serious harvests and income."

## SLIDE 2 — Better crops
**On-screen:** Good seeds, spacing, water, feeding the soil. 🌽
**Say it:** "Modern crop farming starts with quality seeds, correct spacing, irrigation in dry times, and feeding the soil. Add the soil and plant science we learned, and yields jump."

## SLIDE 3 — Protecting the harvest
**On-screen:** Pest control, storage, less waste. 🐛
**Say it:** "Much food is lost to pests and poor storage. Science helps — safe pest control, good drying and storage to stop spoilage and mould. Saving the harvest is as important as growing it."

## SLIDE 4 — Livestock science
**On-screen:** Healthy animals: feed, water, vaccines, shelter. 🐔🐐
**Say it:** "Animal farming — poultry, goats, cattle, fish — thrives on science too: proper feed, clean water, vaccines against disease, and good shelter. Healthy animals mean more meat, eggs, and milk."
**🎓 Go deeper:** Fish farming is **aquaculture** — a fast-growing opportunity in Ghana."

## SLIDE 5 — Technology on the farm
**On-screen:** Apps, weather data, even AI. 📱
**Say it:** "Remember the AI-for-farmers lesson? Apps now give prices, weather, and disease advice. Drones and sensors are arriving. The farmer with a smartphone competes with anyone."

## SLIDE 6 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Farm smart, earn big. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: modern farming uses good seeds, soil care, irrigation, pest and storage management, healthy livestock, and technology. Challenge: pick one crop or animal and list three science-based ways to improve it."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Good seeds + soil + water · 2) Protect harvest & animal health · 3) Use technology.
