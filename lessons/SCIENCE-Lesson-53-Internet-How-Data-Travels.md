# Pillar 1 · Integrated Science — Lesson 53: The Internet & How Data Travels
### "When you send a WhatsApp, it may cross an ocean and back in a second"

> Paste into **NotebookLM** to generate slides/audio. **Say it** = narration/teleprompter.

**Subject:** 1.2 Integrated Science · **Lesson:** 53 · **Audience:** JHS/SHS 🇬🇭 · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Understand what the internet is. 2) Know how data travels. 3) Appreciate the global network.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** Your message to a friend nearby may travel thousands of kilometres. 🌍
**Say it:** "You send a WhatsApp to someone across the street, and the data might shoot across an ocean through undersea cables and back — all in under a second. The internet is a marvel. Let's see how it works."

## SLIDE 2 — What is the internet?
**On-screen:** A giant network of connected computers. 🕸️
**Say it:** "The internet is simply a huge network connecting computers and phones all over the world, so they can share information. No single owner — it's billions of devices talking to each other."

## SLIDE 3 — Data in pieces
**On-screen:** Your message is split into tiny packets. 📦
**Say it:** "Your photo or message is broken into tiny pieces called packets, sent separately across the network, and reassembled at the other end. It's like sending a long letter as many postcards that recombine."
**🎓 Go deeper:** These pieces are **data packets** that find their own routes and rejoin."

## SLIDE 4 — The roads: cables and signals
**On-screen:** Undersea cables, masts, satellites. 🌊
**Say it:** "Data travels through fibre-optic cables — including giant ones under the sea linking continents — plus phone masts and satellites. Ghana connects to the world through these undersea cables landing on our coast."

## SLIDE 5 — Servers store the web
**On-screen:** Websites live on computers called servers. 🖥️
**Say it:** "Every website and app lives on powerful computers called servers. When you open a page, your device asks a server for it, and the data travels back to you. raufhusein.site lives on a server too."

## SLIDE 6 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** A world connected by invisible roads. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: the internet is a global network of computers; data travels in packets; through cables, masts, and satellites; and websites live on servers. Challenge: explain to someone how a WhatsApp message reaches a friend."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Internet = global network · 2) Data travels in packets · 3) Via cables/masts; sites live on servers.

## Quiz
1. What is the internet? *(A global network of connected computers.)* 2. How is your data sent? *(In small packets.)* 3. What is a server? *(A computer that stores websites/apps and serves them.)*
