# Pillar 1 · Integrated Science — Lesson 25: The Nervous System & Brain
### "The fastest messaging network on earth runs inside your head"

> Paste into **NotebookLM** to generate slides/audio. **Say it** = narration/teleprompter.

**Subject:** 1.2 Integrated Science · **Lesson:** 25 · **Audience:** JHS/SHS 🇬🇭 + adults · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Explain how nerves carry messages. 2) Understand reflexes and the senses. 3) Care for your brain and mind.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** Touch something hot and you pull back BEFORE you even think. 🔥✋
**Say it:** "Touch a hot pot and your hand jumps away before your mind even decides. That's your nervous system — the lightning-fast network that runs your whole body. Let's meet the network in your head."

## SLIDE 2 — The control centre
**On-screen:** Brain + spinal cord + nerves. 🧠
**Say it:** "Your nervous system is the brain, the spinal cord down your back, and nerves reaching everywhere. The brain is the boss; nerves are the wires carrying messages in and out at incredible speed."

## SLIDE 3 — Messages as signals
**On-screen:** Nerves send tiny electrical signals. ⚡
**Say it:** "Nerves carry messages as tiny electrical signals — 'the pot is hot', 'move the leg'. Millions zip around every second, letting you sense, think, and move. It's a living computer network."
**🎓 Go deeper:** Nerve cells are called **neurons** — and AI's 'neural networks' are named after them."

## SLIDE 4 — Reflexes: instant protection
**On-screen:** Fast, automatic, life-saving. 🛡️
**Say it:** "A reflex skips the slow thinking brain — the signal loops through the spinal cord for instant action. That's why your hand leaves the heat before you feel pain. Your body protects you faster than thought."

## SLIDE 5 — The five senses
**On-screen:** Sight, hearing, smell, taste, touch. 👁️👂👃👅✋
**Say it:** "Your senses feed the brain information about the world — eyes, ears, nose, tongue, skin. They're how you experience everything. Protecting them, as we learned with light and sound, protects your link to the world."

## SLIDE 6 — Care for your brain & mind
**On-screen:** Sleep · good food · learning · rest from stress. 😴
**Say it:** "The brain needs sleep to work and remember, good food and water to run, and learning to grow stronger. And remember the wellbeing lesson — your mental health is part of brain health. Rest, talk to someone, don't carry stress alone."

## SLIDE 7 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Mind the network that makes you, you. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: the brain, spinal cord, and nerves form your control network; they send electrical signals; reflexes protect you instantly; senses feed the brain; care for it with sleep, food, and rest. Challenge: notice one reflex today and thank your nervous system."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Brain + cord + nerves · 2) Electrical signals + reflexes · 3) Sleep, food, mental care.

## Quiz
1. What three parts make the nervous system? *(Brain, spinal cord, nerves.)* 2. What is a reflex? *(A fast, automatic response that skips conscious thinking.)* 3. What are nerve cells called? *(Neurons.)*
