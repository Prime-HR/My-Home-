## SLIDE 1 — The hook
**On-screen:** The job, the deal, the respect — often goes to the one who speaks well. 🎤
**Say it:** "Don't scroll. Two people know the same thing. One mumbles and looks down; the other speaks clearly and looks up. Guess who gets the job, the customer, the respect? Speaking well is a skill you can learn — and today we start."

## SLIDE 2 — One line
**On-screen:** Confidence is a skill, not a gift you're born with. 💪
**Say it:** "Nobody is born a great speaker. The confident ones simply practised. If they can learn it, so can you."

## SLIDE 3 — Slow down
**On-screen:** Nervous people rush. Calm people pause. ⏸️
**Say it:** "The first secret: slow down. When we're nervous we speak fast and swallow words. Slow your pace, pause between ideas. It makes you sound calm, clear, and in control — even when your heart is racing."

## SLIDE 4 — Beat the fear
**On-screen:** Breathe · prepare · it's normal to shake. 😮‍💨
**Say it:** "Everybody feels fear before speaking — even leaders. Take a slow breath before you start. Know your first sentence by heart. And remember: the audience wants you to do well, not to fail."
**🎓 Go deeper (students):** Fear of speaking is universal; preparation is the cure.

## SLIDE 5 — Structure: beginning, middle, end
**On-screen:** Say what you'll say → say it → sum it up. 🧱
**Say it:** "Don't ramble. Use a simple structure: tell them what you'll talk about, say it in a few clear points, then sum up. People remember speeches that are organised."

## SLIDE 6 — Your body talks too
**On-screen:** Stand tall · look up · don't hide. 🧍🏾
**Say it:** "More than half of speaking is your body. Stand tall, look at people (not the floor), keep your hands calm. A confident body makes your words land harder."

## SLIDE 7 — Practise out loud
**On-screen:** Mirror · record yourself · talk to AI. 🔁
**Say it:** "Practice is everything. Speak to a mirror. Record yourself on your phone and watch it back. Even practise a conversation with an AI. Hearing yourself is how you improve fastest."

## SLIDE 8 — Why it matters
**On-screen:** Your voice opens doors your silence keeps shut. 🚪
**Say it:** "Brilliant ideas die in silent people. The one who can stand and speak clearly leads meetings, wins customers, inspires others. Your voice is one of your most powerful tools — use it."

## SLIDE 9 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Speak up, stand tall, be heard. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: confidence is a learned skill; slow down; breathe and prepare; structure your points; let your body show confidence; practise out loud. Challenge: record a 1-minute talk on your phone about something you love — then watch it once."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Slow down · 2) Beginning–middle–end · 3) Practise out loud.

## Quick Quiz
1. What's one quick way to sound more confident? *(Slow down and pause.)*
2. What simple structure helps a talk? *(Beginning, middle, end / intro, points, summary.)*
3. Name one way to practise speaking. *(Mirror, record yourself, talk to AI.)*

## Teacher version (45 min)
- 0–5: Ask who fears speaking; normalise it.
- 5–15: Teach slowing down, structure, body language.
- 15–35: Each student gives a 1-minute talk on a familiar topic.
- 35–42: Kind peer feedback (one strength, one tip).
- 42–45: Each sets a speaking goal.
