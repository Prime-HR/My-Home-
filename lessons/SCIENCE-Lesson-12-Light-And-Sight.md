# Pillar 1 · Integrated Science — Lesson 12: Light & Sight
### "How light lets you see — and why some eyes need glasses"

> Paste into **NotebookLM** to generate slides/audio. **Say it** = narration/teleprompter.

**Subject:** 1.2 Integrated Science · **Lesson:** 12 · **Audience:** Everyone 🇬🇭 + JHS · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Understand how we see. 2) Explain reflection and colour. 3) Know why glasses help and how to protect eyes.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** You don't see objects — you see light bouncing off them. 👁️
**Say it:** "Strange but true: you never see an object directly. You see light bouncing off it into your eyes. In total darkness, even with open eyes, you see nothing. Light is the messenger of sight. Let's follow it."

## SLIDE 2 — Light travels in straight lines
**On-screen:** Straight rays — that's why shadows form. 📏
**Say it:** "Light moves in straight lines very fast. Because it can't bend around objects, things block it and create shadows. That straight-line travel explains a lot of what you see."

## SLIDE 3 — Reflection
**On-screen:** Light bounces off surfaces. 🪞
**Say it:** "When light hits a surface, it bounces — that's reflection. A mirror reflects it neatly so you see yourself; rough surfaces scatter it. Reflected light from objects is what enters your eyes to let you see them."

## SLIDE 4 — How the eye works
**On-screen:** Lens focuses light onto the back of the eye. 👁️
**Say it:** "Your eye has a lens that bends light to focus a clear picture on the back, which sends it to the brain. When the focus isn't quite right, the picture is blurry — and that's where glasses come in."
**🎓 Go deeper:** A **lens** bends light to focus it; glasses are extra lenses to correct focus."

## SLIDE 5 — Colour
**On-screen:** White light is a mix of colours — the rainbow. 🌈
**Say it:** "White light is actually all colours mixed. A rainbow appears when raindrops split sunlight into its colours. Objects look coloured because they reflect some colours and absorb others."

## SLIDE 6 — Protect your eyes
**On-screen:** Good light · rest · don't stare at the sun or bright screens too long. 🕶️
**Say it:** "Protect this gift: read in good light, rest your eyes from screens, never stare at the sun, and see an eye clinic if vision blurs. Many sight problems are easily corrected when caught early."

## SLIDE 7 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** See how seeing works. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: we see light reflected from objects; light travels straight and makes shadows; the eye's lens focuses it; white light holds all colours; protect your eyes. Challenge: find a reflection and a shadow today and explain each."
**Recap in 3:** 1) We see reflected light · 2) Lens focuses it · 3) White light = all colours.

## Quiz
1. Why can't you see in total darkness? *(No light to reflect into your eyes.)* 2. What does the eye's lens do? *(Focuses light to a clear image.)* 3. What is white light made of? *(All the colours mixed.)*
