# AI For Ghana — Lesson 3: AI Is Everywhere in Your Phone
### "You used AI 20 times today and never noticed"

> **How to use this file:** Paste into **NotebookLM** to generate a slide deck or audio. Each "SLIDE" = one slide. **Say it** = narration / teleprompter.

---

**Lesson:** 3 of 10 (Phase 1 — Foundation) · **Topic:** Everyday AI on your phone
**Audience:** Everyone 🇬🇭 + JHS/SHS (layered) · **Duration:** 30–45-sec cut · 45-min class
**By Rauf Husein · AI For Ghana · raufhusein.site**

**By the end you will be able to:**
1. Spot at least 6 AI features you already use daily.
2. Understand AI is normal, near, and useful — not far-away science fiction.
3. See yourself as an AI user already.

---

## SLIDE 1 — The hook
**On-screen:** You've used AI 20 times today already. Let's count. 📱
**Say it:** "You think AI is in some lab in America. But you've touched it 20 times today on your own phone without noticing. Let's count them together."

## SLIDE 2 — One line
**On-screen:** AI isn't coming. It's already in your hand. ✋
**Say it:** "AI is not the future — it's the present, quietly working inside the apps you use every hour."

## SLIDE 3 — Maps & travel
**On-screen:** Google Maps beats Accra traffic — that's AI. 🗺️
**Say it:** "When Maps finds the fastest route past traffic and predicts how long your trotro ride will take — that's AI studying millions of journeys."

## SLIDE 4 — Your keyboard
**On-screen:** It finishes your words before you type them. ⌨️
**Say it:** "Autocorrect and the word suggestions on your keyboard? AI. It learned how people type and predicts what you want next."

## SLIDE 5 — Your camera & photos
**On-screen:** Face unlock, photo search, beauty mode — all AI. 📸
**Say it:** "Face unlock knows it's you. Your gallery can search 'dog' or 'food' and find the pictures. The camera smooths and brightens automatically. Every bit of it is AI."

## SLIDE 6 — What you watch
**On-screen:** TikTok & YouTube 'For You' = AI reading your taste. 🎬
**Say it:** "TikTok's For You page feels like it reads your mind. It's AI — learning what you like and serving more. That same power is what AIForGhana uses to teach."

## SLIDE 7 — Quiet protectors
**On-screen:** Spam filters, fraud alerts, voice assistants. 🛡️
**Say it:** "AI quietly blocks spam from your inbox, flags strange MoMo transactions as fraud, and powers 'Hey Google'. It's working for you in the background all day."

## SLIDE 8 — Why it matters
**On-screen:** You're already an AI user. Next: become an AI master. 🇬🇭
**Say it:** "Here's the point. You already use AI like an expert without knowing. Imagine what happens when you use it on purpose — to learn, earn, and build. That's our whole mission."

## SLIDE 9 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Count your AIs. ✅ Then teach one person.
**Say it:** "Recap: maps, keyboard, camera, your feed, spam and fraud protection — all AI. Challenge: for the rest of today, notice and count every time you use AI. Share the number."
**Recap in 3:** 1) AI is already in your phone · 2) You use it daily · 3) Now use it on purpose.

## Quick Quiz
1. Name 3 AI features on your phone. *(Maps, autocomplete, face unlock, feed, spam filter…)*
2. How does TikTok decide what to show you? *(AI learning your taste.)*
3. True or false: AI is far away and not in Ghana. *(False — it's in your pocket.)*

## Teacher version (45 min)
- 0–5: "Hands up if you used AI today." Most won't raise — then reveal they all did.
- 5–18: Walk through phone features live.
- 18–30: Scavenger hunt — students find 8 AI features on their own phones.
- 30–40: Discuss: which one would you miss most?
- 40–45: Each writes their personal "AI I use daily" list.
