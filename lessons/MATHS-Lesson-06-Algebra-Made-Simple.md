# Pillar 1 · Mathematics — Lesson 6: Algebra Made Simple
### "The mysterious 'x' is just a clever way to find what you don't know yet"

**Objectives:** 1) Understand what a variable is. 2) Solve a simple equation. 3) Use algebra to solve real problems.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** People fear "x" — but you do algebra at the market every day. 🤔
**Say it:** "The letter 'x' scares students. But when you think 'I have 50 cedis, the bag costs 30, how much is left?' — that's algebra. You already do it. Today we just give it its proper name and power."

## SLIDE 2 — A letter for the unknown
**On-screen:** x = the thing we don't know yet. ❓
**Say it:** "Algebra uses a letter, usually x, to stand for a number we haven't found yet. 'A box of items plus 5 more is 12' becomes x + 5 = 12. The letter is just a placeholder for the answer."
**🎓 Go deeper:** The letter is called a **variable**; the full statement is an **equation**.

## SLIDE 3 — The golden rule
**On-screen:** Keep both sides balanced — like a scale. ⚖️
**Say it:** "An equation is a balance scale. Whatever you do to one side, you must do to the other. To solve x + 5 = 12, take 5 from BOTH sides: x = 7. Balance is the whole secret."

## SLIDE 4 — Solve step by step
**On-screen:** 2x = 10 → x = 5. 🔢
**Say it:** "If 2x = 10, that means two of something equals 10, so each is 5. Divide both sides by 2: x = 5. Undo what was done to x, and you find it."

## SLIDE 5 — Real-life algebra
**On-screen:** Pricing, planning, sharing — solve the unknown. 💡
**Say it:** "Algebra answers real questions: 'If I sell at x cedis and my cost is 30, and I want 20 profit, what is x?' x − 30 = 20, so x = 50. It's a tool for planning your money and your projects."

## SLIDE 6 — Why it's powerful
**On-screen:** Algebra is the language of science, engineering & AI. 🚀
**Say it:** "Every app, bridge, and AI model is built on algebra. The student who befriends 'x' opens the door to the highest-paying careers of the future. Don't fear it — own it."

## SLIDE 7 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Find the unknown, change your future. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: x stands for the unknown; an equation is a balance; solve by doing the same to both sides. Challenge: turn one real money question into an equation and solve it."
**Recap in 3:** 1) x = the unknown · 2) Keep both sides balanced · 3) Undo to solve.
