# AI For Ghana — Lesson 10: How to Use AI as an Entrepreneur
### "Run a business with a free assistant that never sleeps"

> **How to use this file:** Paste into **NotebookLM** to generate a slide deck or audio. Each "SLIDE" = one slide. **Say it** = narration / teleprompter.

---

**Lesson:** 10 of 10 (Phase 1 — Foundation) · **Topic:** AI for business & hustle
**Audience:** Everyone 🇬🇭 (traders, hustlers, youth) + SHS (layered) · **Duration:** 30–45-sec cut · 45-min class
**By Rauf Husein · AI For Ghana · raufhusein.site**

**By the end you will be able to:**
1. Use AI for ideas, marketing, and customer messages.
2. Save time and look professional — for free.
3. Start one small AI-powered business action today.

---

## SLIDE 1 — The hook
**On-screen:** Run your business with a free assistant that never sleeps. 💼🤖
**Say it:** "Imagine hiring a marketer, a copywriter, and a business advisor — for free, working 24 hours. That's what AI can be for any Ghanaian hustler. Let me show you how to put it to work."

## SLIDE 2 — One line
**On-screen:** AI does the talking and thinking tasks — you focus on selling. 🎯
**Say it:** "AI handles the writing, planning, and brainstorming so you spend more time doing what makes money — serving customers and selling."

## SLIDE 3 — Find & test ideas
**On-screen:** "Give me 10 small business ideas for [your town]." 💡
**Say it:** "Starting out? Ask AI for business ideas suited to your area, your budget, your skills. Then ask it to poke holes in your favourite idea before you spend a pesewa."

## SLIDE 4 — Write your marketing
**On-screen:** Captions, adverts, product descriptions — in seconds. 📣
**Say it:** "Ask AI to write your TikTok caption, your WhatsApp advert, your product description — even in a Ghanaian tone. 'Write a fun advert for fresh kelewele for TikTok.' Done. Edit, post, sell."

## SLIDE 5 — Handle customers better
**On-screen:** Draft polite, fast replies to customers. 💬
**Say it:** "Ask AI to help you reply professionally to a complaint, write a follow-up message, or politely chase a payment. Good communication builds trust, and trust builds repeat business."

## SLIDE 6 — Plan and price
**On-screen:** Simple business plans, budgets, pricing help. 📊
**Say it:** "AI can draft a simple business plan, help you budget, and think through pricing — 'If my cost is X, what should I charge to profit?' It's like a business advisor in your pocket."

## SLIDE 7 — Look bigger than you are
**On-screen:** Professional words make a small shop look serious. ✨
**Say it:** "A one-person business can sound polished and trustworthy with AI-written messages, names, and descriptions. Customers can't tell if you're a big company or one determined person — and that levels the field."

## SLIDE 8 — Keep your human edge
**On-screen:** AI assists. Your hustle, judgement & relationships win. 🤝
**Say it:** "AI writes; YOU decide. Your relationships, your reputation, your hustle — that's still the heart of the business. Use AI to move faster, not to lose your personal touch."

## SLIDE 9 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Your free business assistant is ready. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: AI gives ideas, writes your marketing, handles customer messages, and helps you plan and price. Challenge: today, use AI to write one advert or caption for something you sell — and post it."
**Recap in 3:** 1) AI = free business assistant · 2) Ideas, marketing, replies, plans · 3) You keep the human edge.

## Quick Quiz
1. Name 3 business tasks AI can help with. *(Ideas, marketing/captions, customer replies, planning, pricing.)*
2. What should you always keep as the human? *(Judgement, relationships, reputation, hustle.)*
3. Give one AI prompt for marketing. *(e.g., "Write a TikTok advert for my kelewele.")*

## Teacher version (45 min)
- 0–5: "Who sells something or wants to?" Collect businesses.
- 5–18: Demo writing an advert and a business idea list live.
- 18–30: Students use AI to write a caption/advert for a real or imagined business.
- 30–40: Share and critique; discuss keeping the human touch.
- 40–45: Each commits to one AI-powered business action.
