# Pillar 1 · Integrated Science — Lesson 54: Robotics & Automation
### "Machines that sense, think, and act — and the Ghanaians who'll build them"

> Paste into **NotebookLM** to generate slides/audio. **Say it** = narration/teleprompter.

**Subject:** 1.2 Integrated Science · **Lesson:** 54 · **Audience:** JHS/SHS 🇬🇭 · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Understand what a robot is. 2) Know where automation is used. 3) See the careers it opens.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** A machine that can farm, weld, or do surgery without tiring. 🤖
**Say it:** "Imagine a machine that works all day without rest — planting crops, building cars, even helping in surgery. Robots are here, and they need clever people to build and run them. Why not Ghanaians? Let's explore."

## SLIDE 2 — What is a robot?
**On-screen:** A machine that senses, thinks, and acts. ⚙️
**Say it:** "A robot is a machine that can sense its surroundings, make a decision, and act — usually to do a physical task. It combines the sensors, motors, and computing we've learned about."

## SLIDE 3 — The three parts
**On-screen:** Sensors · brain (computer) · movement. 🦾
**Say it:** "Every robot has three things: SENSORS to detect the world (like eyes and touch), a COMPUTER brain to decide, and PARTS that MOVE — motors, wheels, or arms — to act. Sense, think, act."
**🎓 Go deeper:** When AI gives a robot its 'brain', it can learn and adapt."

## SLIDE 4 — Automation everywhere
**On-screen:** Factories, farms, banks, hospitals. 🏭
**Say it:** "Automation means machines doing repetitive jobs automatically — assembling products, packaging food, sorting mail, even ATM machines. It's spreading into farming and healthcare across Africa too."

## SLIDE 5 — Jobs of the future
**On-screen:** Build, program, and maintain robots. 💼
**Say it:** "Remember the 'will AI take my job' lesson — robots change work, but someone must design, program, and repair them. These are among the best careers of your generation, and Ghana needs its own robotics builders."

## SLIDE 6 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Build the machines that build the future. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: a robot senses, thinks, and acts; it has sensors, a computer brain, and moving parts; automation is spreading across industries; and it opens great careers. Challenge: design a simple robot on paper to solve one Ghanaian problem."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Robot = sense, think, act · 2) Sensors + computer + movement · 3) New careers building them.

## Quiz
1. What three things can a robot do? *(Sense, think, act.)* 2. Name the three parts of a robot. *(Sensors, computer brain, moving parts.)* 3. What is automation? *(Machines doing repetitive tasks automatically.)*
