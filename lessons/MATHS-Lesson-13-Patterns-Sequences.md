# Pillar 1 · Mathematics — Lesson 13: Number Patterns & Sequences
### "Spot the pattern, predict the future — from savings to nature"

**Subject:** 1.1 Mathematics · **Lesson:** 13 · **Audience:** Everyone 🇬🇭 + JHS/SHS · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Recognise number patterns. 2) Continue and describe a sequence. 3) Use patterns to plan and predict.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** 2, 4, 8, 16… what comes next? The mind that sees patterns wins. 🔮
**Say it:** "Patterns are everywhere — in your savings, in nature, in music, in code. The person who spots patterns can predict what comes next. That's a superpower in school, business, and AI."

## SLIDE 2 — What is a sequence?
**On-screen:** A list of numbers following a rule. 🔢
**Say it:** "A sequence is a list of numbers that follow a rule. Find the rule and you can continue it forever. 2, 4, 6, 8 follows 'add 2'."

## SLIDE 3 — Two big types
**On-screen:** Add the same (5,10,15) · Multiply the same (2,4,8). ➕✖️
**Say it:** "Two common patterns. One adds the same amount each time — 5, 10, 15, 20. The other multiplies — 2, 4, 8, 16, doubling each time. Spotting which one is the key."
**🎓 Go deeper:** Adding the same = **arithmetic**; multiplying the same = **geometric** (this is how savings with interest grow)."

## SLIDE 4 — Patterns predict money
**On-screen:** Save ₵50 weekly → see your future total. 💰
**Say it:** "Sequences plan your money. Save 50 cedis every week and the pattern shows exactly where you'll be in a year. Interest creates a multiplying pattern that grows faster and faster — the saver's friend."

## SLIDE 5 — Patterns in nature & code
**On-screen:** Petals, shells, music, algorithms. 🌿
**Say it:** "Nature loves patterns — the spiral of a shell, the arrangement of seeds. Computers and AI run on patterns too. Learning to see them connects maths to the real and digital world."

## SLIDE 6 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** See the rule, see the future. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: a sequence follows a rule; common ones add or multiply the same amount; patterns predict savings and appear in nature and code. Challenge: make a savings sequence for the next 8 weeks and find your total."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Find the rule · 2) Add-the-same vs multiply-the-same · 3) Patterns predict.
