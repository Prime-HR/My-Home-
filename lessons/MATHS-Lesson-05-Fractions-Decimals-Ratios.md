# Pillar 1 · Mathematics — Lesson 5: Fractions, Decimals & Ratios
### "How to share fairly, mix correctly, and never be cheated on a part"

**Objectives:** 1) Read fractions & decimals. 2) Convert between them. 3) Use ratios to share and mix fairly.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** "Half of the profit" — but do you know how to split it FAIRLY? ➗
**Say it:** "Three friends start a business. One says 'share it half-half-half' — but that's three halves, which is impossible. Most quarrels over money are really quarrels over fractions and ratios. Let's master sharing."

## SLIDE 2 — Fractions = parts of a whole
**On-screen:** 1/2, 3/4, 1/3 — top is how many, bottom is out of how many. 🍕
**Say it:** "A fraction shows parts of a whole. 3/4 means 3 parts out of 4. The bottom number tells you how many equal parts; the top tells you how many you have."

## SLIDE 3 — Decimals are fractions in disguise
**On-screen:** 0.5 = 1/2 · 0.25 = 1/4 · 0.75 = 3/4. 🔢
**Say it:** "Decimals are just another way to write fractions. Half a cedi is 0.50. A quarter is 0.25. Your MoMo and prices use decimals every day."

## SLIDE 4 — Convert with ease
**On-screen:** Fraction → decimal: divide top by bottom. ➗
**Say it:** "To turn 3/4 into a decimal, divide 3 by 4 — that's 0.75. To go back, 0.75 is 75 out of 100, which simplifies to 3/4. Practice a few and it becomes natural."
**🎓 Go deeper:** A percentage is just a fraction out of 100 — 75% = 0.75 = 3/4."

## SLIDE 5 — Ratios: sharing in proportion
**On-screen:** Share ₵100 in ratio 3:2 → ₵60 and ₵40. 🤝
**Say it:** "A ratio shares things by proportion. To split 100 cedis in the ratio 3 to 2: add the parts (3+2=5), divide 100 by 5 to get 20, then give 3×20=60 and 2×20=40. Fair, clear, no quarrel."

## SLIDE 6 — Mixing & recipes
**On-screen:** Cement 1:2:4 · drinks · cooking. 🧱🍲
**Say it:** "Ratios run real life — concrete mixed 1:2:4, a recipe scaled up for a party, a drink diluted correctly. Get the ratio wrong and you waste money or ruin the result."

## SLIDE 7 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Share fair, mix right. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: fractions are parts of a whole; decimals are the same in another form; ratios share by proportion. Challenge: split a real amount among people using a ratio today."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Fractions = parts · 2) Decimal = divide top by bottom · 3) Ratio = share by proportion.
