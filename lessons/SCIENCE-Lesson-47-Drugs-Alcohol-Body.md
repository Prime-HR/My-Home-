# Pillar 1 · Integrated Science — Lesson 47: Drugs, Alcohol & Your Body
### "The science of what these substances really do — so you can choose wisely"

> Paste into **NotebookLM** to generate slides/audio. **Say it** = narration/teleprompter.

**Subject:** 1.2 Integrated Science · **Lesson:** 47 · **Audience:** JHS/SHS 🇬🇭 (health education) · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Understand how these substances affect the body. 2) Know the real risks. 3) Build confidence to make healthy choices.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** They're sold as 'fun' — but here's what they actually do inside you. 🧠
**Say it:** "Friends, music, and adverts may make alcohol and drugs look harmless or cool. But science shows what really happens inside your body. Knowing the facts gives you the power to choose your own path."

## SLIDE 2 — They affect the brain
**On-screen:** Drugs and alcohol change how the brain works. ⚡
**Say it:** "Remember the nervous system lesson? These substances interfere with the brain's signals — slowing thinking, blurring judgement, and changing mood. That's why they cause accidents and bad decisions."

## SLIDE 3 — Alcohol
**On-screen:** Slows the body; harms the liver over time. 🍺
**Say it:** "Alcohol slows reactions and judgement — dangerous for driving and choices. Over time, heavy drinking damages the liver and brain. It's a leading cause of road deaths and family harm."

## SLIDE 4 — Other drugs & addiction
**On-screen:** The brain can get trapped — that's addiction. 🔒
**Say it:** "Many drugs hijack the brain's reward system, making a person crave more and more — addiction. What starts as 'just once' can trap someone, harming health, school, money, and relationships. The grip is real and hard to break."
**🎓 Go deeper:** Addiction is a medical condition of the brain — not simply 'weak will'."

## SLIDE 5 — Tobacco
**On-screen:** Smoking scars the lungs and heart. 🚬
**Say it:** "Tobacco harms the lungs and heart we studied, causing cancer and breathing disease. There is no safe level of smoking — and it's addictive, which is why it's best never to start."

## SLIDE 6 — Your power to choose
**On-screen:** Strong people walk their own path. 💪
**Say it:** "True strength is choosing your own future over a moment's pressure. Surround yourself with people who lift you, find healthy ways to enjoy life, and if you or a friend is struggling, talk to a trusted adult or health worker — help exists."

## SLIDE 7 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Know the facts, own your choices. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: drugs and alcohol disturb the brain; alcohol harms judgement and the liver; drugs can cause addiction; tobacco damages lungs and heart; you have the power to choose. Challenge: plan one confident response for when you're offered something you don't want."
**Recap in 3:** 1) They harm the brain & body · 2) Addiction is a real trap · 3) You have the power to choose.

## Quiz
1. What body part do drugs and alcohol mainly affect? *(The brain/nervous system.)* 2. What is addiction? *(A brain condition causing strong cravings/dependence.)* 3. What does smoking damage? *(Lungs and heart.)*
