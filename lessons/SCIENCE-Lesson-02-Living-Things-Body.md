## SLIDE 1 — The hook
**On-screen:** Your body does a billion things a second — and you never thank it. 🫀
**Say it:** "Don't scroll. Right now, without you doing anything, your heart is pumping, your lungs breathing, your blood fighting germs, your brain running the whole show. The most advanced machine in Ghana is YOU. Let's meet it."

## SLIDE 2 — What makes something living?
**On-screen:** It moves, grows, feeds, breathes, senses, reproduces. 🌱
**Say it:** "How do we know something is alive? Living things share signs: they feed, grow, move, breathe, respond to their surroundings, and reproduce. A stone does none of these; a mango tree does them all."
**🎓 Go deeper (students):** Remember **MRS GREN** — Movement, Respiration, Sensitivity, Growth, Reproduction, Excretion, Nutrition.

## SLIDE 3 — Everything is built from cells
**On-screen:** Cells = the tiny bricks of all life. 🧱
**Say it:** "Every living thing is built from tiny units called cells — billions of them in you, far too small to see. Cells are the bricks; from them come your skin, blood, muscles, and brain."

## SLIDE 4 — Your body's teams (systems)
**On-screen:** Digestive · Circulatory · Respiratory · Nervous. ⚙️
**Say it:** "Your body works as teams called systems. Digestive — turns banku into energy. Circulatory — blood delivers it everywhere. Respiratory — lungs bring in oxygen. Nervous — brain and nerves run the controls. Each team has a job, all working together."

## SLIDE 5 — Food becomes YOU
**On-screen:** What you eat literally builds your body. 🍲
**Say it:** "Your body is rebuilt from what you eat. Good food — beans, fish, kontomire, fruits — gives strong cells. Junk and too much sugar give a weak, sick body. You are, quite literally, what you eat."

## SLIDE 6 — Why you fall sick
**On-screen:** Germs vs your body's soldiers. 🦠🛡️
**Say it:** "Sickness often comes from tiny germs — bacteria and viruses — invading your body. But you have soldiers: your immune system. Clean water, good food, rest, and vaccines keep your soldiers strong so they win the battle."

## SLIDE 7 — Protect the machine
**On-screen:** Hygiene · clean water · rest · exercise. 🧼
**Say it:** "Simple habits protect this priceless machine: wash your hands, drink clean water, sleep, move your body. Most common illnesses in Ghana are prevented by these basics, not by expensive medicine."

## SLIDE 8 — Why it matters
**On-screen:** Understand your body, protect your life. 🇬🇭
**Say it:** "When you understand how your body works, you take better care of it, you spot problems early, and you don't fall for fake cures. Knowledge of your own body is the most personal science of all."

## SLIDE 9 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Respect the machine that carries your dreams. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: living things share signs of life; all life is made of cells; your body's systems work as teams; good food and hygiene keep them strong. Challenge: name the system at work next time you eat, run, or breathe deeply — and thank your body."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Life signs (MRS GREN) · 2) Cells build everything · 3) Systems work as teams.

## Quick Quiz
1. Name 3 signs of living things. *(Feeding, growing, moving, breathing, reproducing…)*
2. What are the tiny units all life is built from? *(Cells.)*
3. Name one body system and its job. *(e.g., Respiratory — brings in oxygen.)*

## Teacher version (45 min)
- 0–5: "What is your body doing right now without you trying?"
- 5–18: Teach signs of life, cells, and the main systems.
- 18–30: Activity — students map one system on a body outline.
- 30–40: Link food and hygiene to healthy systems.
- 40–45: Each names one habit to protect their body.
