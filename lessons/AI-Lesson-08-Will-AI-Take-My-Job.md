# AI For Ghana — Lesson 8: Will AI Take My Job?
### "The honest answer every Ghanaian needs to hear"

> **How to use this file:** Paste into **NotebookLM** to generate a slide deck or audio. Each "SLIDE" = one slide. **Say it** = narration / teleprompter.

---

**Lesson:** 8 of 10 (Phase 1 — Foundation) · **Topic:** AI and the future of work
**Audience:** Everyone 🇬🇭 (workers, youth) + JHS/SHS (layered) · **Duration:** 30–45-sec cut · 45-min class
**By Rauf Husein · AI For Ghana · raufhusein.site**

**By the end you will be able to:**
1. Give the honest answer to "will AI take my job?".
2. Know which skills keep you valuable.
3. Make a simple plan to stay ahead.

---

## SLIDE 1 — The hook
**On-screen:** Will AI take YOUR job? Let's be honest. 😬
**Say it:** "Everybody's afraid to ask it, so let me say it plainly. Will AI take your job? I won't lie to you or hype you. Here is the honest answer."

## SLIDE 2 — The real answer
**On-screen:** AI won't replace you. But a person USING AI might. 🎯
**Say it:** "AI by itself won't replace most people. But a person who uses AI well may replace the person who refuses to. The danger isn't the machine — it's being left behind by those who learn it."

## SLIDE 3 — What changes
**On-screen:** Boring, repetitive tasks → AI. 🤖
**Say it:** "AI is best at repetitive, predictable tasks — copying data, basic writing, simple calculations. Those parts of many jobs will be done faster by AI. That's not all bad — it frees humans for better work."

## SLIDE 4 — What stays human
**On-screen:** Creativity · care · leadership · trust · hands-on skill. ❤️
**Say it:** "Things AI struggles to replace: genuine creativity, caring for people, leading a team, building trust, and skilled hands — the mechanic, the seamstress, the nurse, the teacher who inspires. Human strengths become MORE valuable, not less."

## SLIDE 5 — The new rule of work
**On-screen:** Your value = your skill + how well you use AI. ➕
**Say it:** "The new formula is simple. Your value tomorrow equals your human skill plus your ability to use AI as a tool. Master both and you're not threatened — you're unstoppable."

## SLIDE 6 — Honest about the hard part
**On-screen:** Some jobs will shrink. That's real. 📉
**Say it:** "I won't sugar-coat it. Some roles will shrink or change a lot. Pretending otherwise helps no one. The answer isn't fear — it's preparation. The earlier you adapt, the safer you are."

## SLIDE 7 — Your plan to stay ahead
**On-screen:** Learn AI tools · sharpen human skills · keep learning. 🚀
**Say it:** "Three moves. One: learn to use AI tools in your field. Two: deepen the human skills AI can't copy. Three: become a lifelong learner — the world will keep changing, and so must you."

## SLIDE 8 — Why Ghana can win
**On-screen:** Young nation + AI skills = huge advantage. 🇬🇭
**Say it:** "Ghana is young, hungry, and adaptable. While older economies struggle to retrain, our youth can leap straight into the AI age. This can be our advantage — if we start now."

## SLIDE 9 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Don't fear AI. Out-learn it. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: AI won't replace you, but someone using AI might; boring tasks go to AI; human skills rise; your value is skill plus AI. Challenge: name one AI tool you'll learn for your work or studies this month."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Person-with-AI beats person-without · 2) Human skills rise · 3) Keep learning.

## Quick Quiz
1. What's the honest answer to "will AI take my job?" *(AI won't, but a person using AI might.)*
2. Name 2 skills AI can't easily replace. *(Creativity, care, leadership, trust, hands-on skill.)*
3. What's the new formula for your value? *(Human skill + ability to use AI.)*

## Teacher version (45 min)
- 0–5: Anonymous poll — "Are you afraid AI will take jobs?"
- 5–18: Teach the honest answer and the new formula.
- 18–30: Activity — list tasks in a chosen job; mark which AI could do vs which stay human.
- 30–40: Each writes a personal "stay ahead" plan.
- 40–45: Share plans; encourage.
