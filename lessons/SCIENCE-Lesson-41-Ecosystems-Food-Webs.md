# Pillar 1 · Integrated Science — Lesson 41: Ecosystems & Food Webs
### "Remove one creature and the whole web can collapse"

> Paste into **NotebookLM** to generate slides/audio. **Say it** = narration/teleprompter.

**Subject:** 1.2 Integrated Science · **Lesson:** 41 · **Audience:** JHS/SHS 🇬🇭 · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Explain an ecosystem. 2) Build a simple food web. 3) See why balance matters.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** Kill all the frogs and you may get MORE mosquitoes — and more malaria. 🐸🦟
**Say it:** "Everything in nature is connected. Remove one creature and you can trigger a chain that harms even humans. Let's see the invisible web that keeps Ghana's nature — and us — in balance."

## SLIDE 2 — What is an ecosystem?
**On-screen:** Living things + their environment, working together. 🌳
**Say it:** "An ecosystem is a community of living things — plants, animals, microbes — together with their surroundings of soil, water, and air. A forest, a river, even a farm is an ecosystem."

## SLIDE 3 — Food chains and webs
**On-screen:** Sun → plants → animals → predators. 🔗
**Say it:** "Energy flows from the sun to plants, to the animals that eat them, to the predators that eat those. Many chains crossing form a food web. Everything is somebody's meal or somebody's helper."
**🎓 Go deeper:** Plants are **producers**; animals are **consumers**; microbes are **decomposers** that recycle waste."

## SLIDE 4 — Balance is delicate
**On-screen:** Remove one link, the web shakes. ⚖️
**Say it:** "Each creature has a role. Remove the frogs and insects multiply; remove the predators and prey overrun the land; remove the bees and crops fail. Balance keeps everyone fed and safe."

## SLIDE 5 — Humans in the web
**On-screen:** We depend on it — and can break it. 🇬🇭
**Say it:** "We are part of the web too. Our food, clean air, and water come from healthy ecosystems. Galamsey, overfishing, and deforestation tear the web — and it tears back at us."

## SLIDE 6 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Protect the web that feeds you. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: an ecosystem is living things plus their environment; energy flows in food webs; every creature has a role; breaking the balance harms us. Challenge: draw a simple food web for a Ghanaian farm or river."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Ecosystem = life + environment · 2) Food webs move energy · 3) Balance protects all.

## Quiz
