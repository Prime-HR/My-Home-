# Pillar 1 · Integrated Science — Lesson 39: The Universe & Space
### "The satellites guiding your Maps and MoMo are part of Africa's space future"

> Paste into **NotebookLM** to generate slides/audio. **Say it** = narration/teleprompter.

**Subject:** 1.2 Integrated Science · **Lesson:** 39 · **Audience:** JHS/SHS 🇬🇭 · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Know our place in space. 2) Understand how satellites serve daily life. 3) See Africa's space future.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** The Maps that guide your trotro talk to machines in space. 🛰️
**Say it:** "When Google Maps finds your route, it's talking to satellites orbiting far above Earth. Space isn't only far-away stars — it runs your daily life. And Africa is reaching for it. Let's look up."

## SLIDE 2 — Our place in space
**On-screen:** Earth → Solar System → Galaxy → Universe. 🌌
**Say it:** "Earth is one planet circling the Sun, with seven others, in our Solar System. The Sun is one of billions of stars in our galaxy, the Milky Way — and there are billions of galaxies. We are small, and that is humbling and inspiring."

## SLIDE 3 — Stars and the Sun
**On-screen:** The Sun is our nearest star. ⭐
**Say it:** "Stars are giant balls of burning gas, like our Sun — the closest one, which gives us light, heat, and life. The tiny stars you see at night are distant suns, some far bigger than ours."
**🎓 Go deeper:** Planets don't make light — they reflect the Sun's, like our moon."

## SLIDE 4 — Satellites serve you daily
**On-screen:** GPS · TV · phones · weather. 📡
**Say it:** "Humans have launched thousands of satellites. They give us GPS for Maps, beam TV and phone signals, watch the weather, and monitor crops and floods. The space above us is busy working for us."

## SLIDE 5 — Africa in space
**On-screen:** African nations now launch satellites too. 🚀🌍
**Say it:** "This isn't only for rich nations. African countries — including Ghana, which launched its first satellite, GhanaSat-1 — are entering space to monitor farms, coasts, and resources. The sky belongs to us too."

## SLIDE 6 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Look up — your future may be there. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: Earth is one planet among billions of stars and galaxies; stars are suns; satellites run GPS, phones, and weather; and Africa, including Ghana, is now reaching space. Challenge: tonight, find one star or planet and remember it's a distant sun or world."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Earth → Solar System → Galaxy → Universe · 2) Satellites serve daily life · 3) Africa is in space too.
