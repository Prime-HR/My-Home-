## SLIDE 1 — The hook
**On-screen:** Ice, water, steam — same thing, three faces. And it explains your light bill. 🧊💧♨️
**Say it:** "Don't scroll. Ice, water, and steam are the SAME substance wearing three different clothes. Understand that, and you understand cooking, weather, and even why electricity costs what it does. Let's unlock it."

## SLIDE 2 — Everything is matter
**On-screen:** Matter = anything that takes up space & has weight. 🌍
**Say it:** "Matter is everything you can touch — water, air, stone, your body. If it takes up space and has weight, it's matter. And it comes in three main states."

## SLIDE 3 — Solid, liquid, gas
**On-screen:** Solid (fixed) · Liquid (flows) · Gas (spreads). 🧊
**Say it:** "Solids hold their shape, like ice or a stone. Liquids flow and take the shape of the container, like water or oil. Gases spread everywhere, like steam or air. Same substance can move between all three."
**🎓 Go deeper (students):** Heating and cooling cause **changes of state** — melting, boiling, freezing, evaporation, condensation."

## SLIDE 4 — Heat changes everything
**On-screen:** Add heat → it melts/boils. Remove heat → it freezes. 🔥❄️
**Say it:** "Heat is the key. Add heat to ice and it melts to water, then boils to steam. Remove heat and steam becomes water, then ice. This is exactly why fish dries in the sun and water disappears from a bucket — evaporation."

## SLIDE 5 — What is energy?
**On-screen:** Energy = the ability to do work or make things happen. ⚡
**Say it:** "Energy is what makes things happen — movement, heat, light, sound. It comes in forms: the heat from fire, the light from the sun, the electricity in your wires, the chemical energy in food that powers your body."

## SLIDE 6 — Electricity, simply
**On-screen:** Electricity = energy that flows through wires to do work. 💡
**Say it:** "Electricity is energy flowing through wires to give us light, power our phones, and run machines. In Ghana, much of it comes from the Akosombo Dam — moving water spinning turbines — and from other sources too."

## SLIDE 7 — Why dumsor & high bills
**On-screen:** Demand vs supply · wasted energy = money lost. 🔌
**Say it:** "When the country needs more power than it can supply, we get dumsor. And at home, your bill rises when energy is wasted — appliances left on, old bulbs, irons and heaters running long. Energy is money flowing out of your pocket."

## SLIDE 8 — Save energy, save money
**On-screen:** LED bulbs · switch off · unplug · use daylight. 💰
**Say it:** "Simple science saves cedis: use efficient LED bulbs, switch off and unplug what you're not using, iron clothes in one batch, use daylight. Small habits, real savings, every single month."

## SLIDE 9 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Understand energy, control your bill. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: matter is solid, liquid, or gas, and heat moves it between them; energy makes things happen; electricity is flowing energy; waste it and you pay more. Challenge: find one energy waster in your home today and fix it."
**Recap in 3:** 1) 3 states of matter · 2) Heat changes state · 3) Save energy = save money.
