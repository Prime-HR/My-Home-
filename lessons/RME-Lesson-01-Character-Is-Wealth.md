# Pillar 1 · Religious & Moral Education — Lesson 1: Character — The Wealth No One Can Steal

## SLIDE 1 — The hook
**On-screen:** Talent opens the door. Character decides if you stay. 🚪
**Say it:** "Don't scroll. You can be the most talented person in the room and still lose everything — because of character. A liar, a thief, a proud person: their gift takes them up, but their character brings them down. Today, the wealth no one can ever steal from you."

## SLIDE 2 — One line
**On-screen:** Character = who you are when no one is watching. 👁️
**Say it:** "Character is simply who you are when nobody is watching and there's no reward. That hidden you is the real you — and it decides your whole life."

## SLIDE 3 — Ghana's faiths agree on the basics
**On-screen:** Christianity · Islam · African Traditional values. 🤝
**Say it:** "We are a nation of Christians, Muslims, and traditional believers — and on the things that matter most, they agree: honesty, respect, kindness, hard work, and caring for others. Good character is common ground for us all."

## SLIDE 4 — Honesty
**On-screen:** A good name is worth more than gold. 💎
**Say it:** "Honesty builds trust, and trust is the currency of every relationship and business. A lie may win today but it destroys your name tomorrow. In a small place where everyone knows everyone, your name is your fortune."

## SLIDE 5 — Respect
**On-screen:** Respect elders, respect peers, respect yourself. 🙏
**Say it:** "Respect is at the heart of Ghanaian culture — for elders, for one another, and for yourself. It costs nothing and earns everything. The respectful young person is the one doors open for."

## SLIDE 6 — Hard work & integrity
**On-screen:** Earn it. Shortcuts have a price. 💪
**Say it:** "Real success is earned. The shortcut — cheating, stealing, '419' — always carries a hidden bill that comes due. Integrity means doing right even when wrong is easier. That is strength."

## SLIDE 7 — Ubuntu: I am because we are
**On-screen:** Your success should lift others too. 🌍
**Say it:** "African wisdom says: I am because we are. Your blessing is meant to bless your family and community too. The one who climbs and then lifts others — that is true greatness."

## SLIDE 8 — Why it matters (even in the AI age)
**On-screen:** New tools, same timeless values. 🤖❤️
**Say it:** "Technology changes fast, but character never goes out of date. AI can write your words, but it can't give you integrity. In a world of powerful tools, good character is what makes you trustworthy — and trust is everything."

## SLIDE 9 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Build the wealth no one can steal. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: character beats talent alone; our faiths share honesty, respect, hard work, and care; and good character matters more than ever in the AI age. Challenge: pick ONE value — honesty, respect, or kindness — and practise it deliberately every day this week."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Character > talent alone · 2) Honesty, respect, hard work, Ubuntu · 3) Values never go out of date.

## Quick Quiz
1. What is character? *(Who you are when no one is watching.)*
2. Name 3 values shared across Ghana's faiths. *(Honesty, respect, kindness, hard work, care for others.)*
3. What does "Ubuntu" / "I am because we are" mean? *(My success is tied to and should lift my community.)*

## Teacher version (45 min)
- 0–5: "Who would you trust with your money, and why?" Discuss character.
- 5–18: Explore the shared values across faiths respectfully.
- 18–30: Scenarios — students decide the right action and explain.
- 30–40: Discuss Ubuntu and lifting others.
- 40–45: Each chooses one value to practise this week.
