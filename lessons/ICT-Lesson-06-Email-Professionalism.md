# Pillar 1 · ICT — Lesson 6: Email & Being Professional Online
### "Your email and your online name open doors — or close them"

---

## SLIDE 1 — The hook
**On-screen:** "sweetboy_chaledext@..." just lost you the job. 📧😬
**Say it:** "An employer gets two CVs. One email reads 'sharpgirl_baby2'. The other reads 'ama.mensah'. Before reading a word, they've already judged. Your email and your online name speak before you do. Let's make them speak well."

## SLIDE 2 — Get a professional email
**On-screen:** firstname.lastname@gmail.com 📬
**Say it:** "Create a free Gmail using your real name — firstname dot lastname. Simple, clean, serious. Keep your fun nickname for friends; use the professional one for jobs, school, and business."

## SLIDE 3 — The 4 parts of a good email
**On-screen:** Subject · Greeting · Message · Sign-off. ✉️
**Say it:** "Every good email has four parts. A clear SUBJECT so they know what it's about. A GREETING — 'Dear Sir/Madam' or 'Hello Mr Mensah'. A short, clear MESSAGE. And a SIGN-OFF — 'Kind regards, your name'. Four parts, every time."

## SLIDE 4 — Write it clear and short
**On-screen:** Say who you are, what you want, what's next. 🎯
**Say it:** "Don't tell a long story. Three things: who you are, what you want, and what you'd like them to do. Busy people respect short, clear messages. Read it once before you send."

## SLIDE 5 — Manners matter
**On-screen:** Polite · no shouting (CAPS) · reply within a day. 🙏
**Say it:** "Be polite — 'please' and 'thank you' still win in business. Don't type in capitals. And reply within a day when you can; fast, courteous replies build a strong reputation."

## SLIDE 6 — Your whole online name
**On-screen:** Employers Google you. What will they find? 🔍
**Say it:** "It's bigger than email. Before hiring, many people search your name and check your social media. What you post becomes your public CV. Post like your future boss is watching — because they might be."

## SLIDE 7 — Clean it up & build it up
**On-screen:** Remove the messy. Add the proud. 🧹➕
**Say it:** "Two moves. Remove or hide posts you wouldn't show an employer. Then build a positive presence — share what you're learning, like this AI journey. Turn your profile into proof of who you're becoming."

## SLIDE 8 — Let AI help you write
**On-screen:** Ask AI to draft a polite email — then make it yours. 🤖
**Say it:** "Nervous about wording an important email? Ask an AI to draft it politely and professionally, then edit it to sound like you. No Ghanaian should lose an opportunity over a few words again."

## SLIDE 9 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Look professional, get treated professionally. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: get a name-based email; use subject, greeting, message, sign-off; be polite and clear; keep your whole online presence clean. Challenge: create your professional email today and send one proper email."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Name-based email · 2) Subject, greeting, message, sign-off · 3) Your posts are your public CV.

## Teacher version (45 min)
- 0–5: Show two email addresses — which gets the interview?
- 5–18: Teach the 4 parts; write a sample email together.
- 18–32: Students create a professional email and draft a real one (e.g., to a school/employer).
- 32–42: Discuss online presence; what to clean, what to build.
- 42–45: Each sends one proper email.
