# Pillar 1 · Integrated Science — Lesson 33: Carbon & Fuels
### "From charcoal to petrol — the energy we burn, and its hidden cost"

> Paste into **NotebookLM** to generate slides/audio. **Say it** = narration/teleprompter.

**Subject:** 1.2 Integrated Science · **Lesson:** 33 · **Audience:** JHS/SHS 🇬🇭 · **By Rauf Husein · AIForGhana · raufhusein.site**

**Objectives:** 1) Understand fuels and combustion. 2) Know why fuels release carbon dioxide. 3) Link burning to climate and cleaner choices.

## SLIDE 1 — Hook
**On-screen:** Charcoal, petrol, gas — all the same trick: storing the sun's energy. 🔥
**Say it:** "From the coalpot cooking your food to the petrol moving your trotro, we burn fuels every day. They all do the same thing — release stored energy. But that fire has a hidden cost. Let's understand both."

## SLIDE 2 — What is a fuel?
**On-screen:** A store of energy we release by burning. 🪵
**Say it:** "A fuel is a substance that stores energy we can release, usually by burning. Wood, charcoal, petrol, diesel, and gas are all fuels. Burning unlocks the energy to cook, move, and power machines."

## SLIDE 3 — Combustion
**On-screen:** Fuel + oxygen → heat + light + carbon dioxide. 💨
**Say it:** "Burning is a chemical reaction called combustion: fuel joins with oxygen to release heat and light — and produce carbon dioxide gas. Remember the reactions lesson: new substances form, and energy comes out."
**🎓 Go deeper:** Most fuels are **carbon-based**; burning them releases **carbon dioxide (CO₂)**."

## SLIDE 4 — The hidden cost
**On-screen:** CO₂ traps heat → climate change. 🌡️
**Say it:** "Here's the catch. The carbon dioxide from all our burning is the very gas that traps heat and drives climate change, as we learned. The world's fuels are warming the planet — including the floods and heat in Ghana."

## SLIDE 5 — Burning safely
**On-screen:** Ventilation · never indoors with poor air. ⚠️
**Say it:** "Burning also has a direct danger: incomplete burning makes carbon monoxide, a poison you can't smell. Never run generators or coalpots in closed rooms. Always allow fresh air. This warning saves lives."

## SLIDE 6 — Cleaner choices
**On-screen:** Efficient stoves · gas over wood · renewables. ♻️
**Say it:** "We can burn smarter: efficient stoves use less charcoal, gas burns cleaner than wood, and renewable energy avoids burning altogether. Each cleaner choice means healthier air and a cooler planet."

## SLIDE 7 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Use the fire, mind the cost. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: fuels store energy released by combustion; burning needs oxygen and makes carbon dioxide; that CO₂ drives climate change; burn safely with fresh air; choose cleaner options. Challenge: name three fuels used in your home and a cleaner alternative for one."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Fuels store energy · 2) Combustion makes CO₂ · 3) Burn safely; choose cleaner.
