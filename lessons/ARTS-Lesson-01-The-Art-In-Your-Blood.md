## SLIDE 1 — The hook
**On-screen:** Your ancestors coded messages into cloth long before computers. 🧵💡
**Say it:** "Don't scroll. Before computers, Ghanaians were storing whole proverbs and meanings inside cloth and symbols — Kente colours and Adinkra signs. We are a nation of artists. And today, art isn't just culture — it's a career that pays. Let me show you the genius in your blood."

## SLIDE 2 — One line
**On-screen:** Art is how a people speak their soul. 🎨
**Say it:** "Art — cloth, music, dance, drama, craft — is how a people express who they are. And Ghana is one of the most creative nations on earth."

## SLIDE 3 — Kente: cloth that talks
**On-screen:** Every colour and pattern has meaning. 🟡🟢🔴
**Say it:** "Kente isn't just beautiful — it's a language. Gold for royalty and wealth, green for growth, blue for peace. Each pattern has a name and a message. Our weavers were designers and storytellers in one."

## SLIDE 4 — Adinkra: symbols of wisdom
**On-screen:** Sankofa, Gye Nyame, Dwennimmen… 🪧
**Say it:** "Adinkra symbols turn proverbs into pictures. 'Gye Nyame' — except for God — speaks of His supremacy. 'Dwennimmen', the ram's horns, means strength with humility. A single symbol can carry a whole philosophy."
**🎓 Go deeper (students):** Adinkra is visual communication — design with deep meaning, like a logo with a soul.

## SLIDE 5 — Music & dance: our global gift
**On-screen:** Highlife, Hiplife, Afrobeats, Azonto. 🎶💃
**Say it:** "From Highlife to Azonto to today's Afrobeats wave, Ghanaian rhythm moves the whole world. Our music and dance are not just fun — they are powerful cultural exports carrying our name everywhere."

## SLIDE 6 — Art tells the truth
**On-screen:** Drama, story, and craft teach and challenge. 🎭
**Say it:** "Art isn't only entertainment. Drama and stories teach values, challenge wrongs, and heal communities. A song can do what a thousand lectures cannot."

## SLIDE 7 — Art is now a real career
**On-screen:** Designers, animators, musicians, content creators. 💼
**Say it:** "Here's the modern truth: the creative economy is booming. Graphic designers, fashion brands, animators, musicians, film-makers, content creators — Ghanaians are earning real money doing what they love. Add digital tools and AI, and your creativity can reach the world from your bedroom."

## SLIDE 8 — Why it matters
**On-screen:** Our culture is our identity AND our gold. 🇬🇭
**Say it:** "Our art carries our identity and can carry our economy. The young person who masters both tradition and modern tools — Kente knowledge plus digital design — holds something truly valuable and uniquely Ghanaian."

## SLIDE 9 — Recap + challenge
**On-screen:** Create. Ghana runs in your hands. ✅
**Say it:** "Recap: Kente and Adinkra carry meaning; our music and dance move the world; and art is a serious modern career. Challenge: create one small thing today — a drawing, a beat, a design, a poem — and share it proudly."
**Recap in 3:** 1) Kente & Adinkra = meaning · 2) Our music moves the world · 3) Art is a real career.

## Quick Quiz
1. What does Kente cloth communicate? *(Meanings through colour and pattern — e.g., gold = royalty.)*
2. What is Adinkra? *(Symbols that express proverbs/wisdom.)*
3. Name 2 Ghanaian music or dance forms. *(Highlife, Hiplife, Afrobeats, Azonto.)*

## Teacher version (45 min)
- 0–5: Show Kente and an Adinkra symbol — ask what students think they mean.
- 5–18: Teach the meanings of colours and key symbols.
- 18–32: Create — each student designs their own symbol with a meaning.
- 32–42: Share designs; discuss creative careers.
- 42–45: Each names one creative skill they'll explore.
