```
THREAD: "Why software built for Africa by non-Africans usually fails 🧵

1/ Most enterprise software sold to African businesses was designed for
   Western infrastructure. Here's why that's a problem...

2/ Connectivity. Most software assumes stable internet. Reality in Ghana:
   load shedding, weak signal, mobile data caps. Our systems work offline first.

3/ Currency & tax rules. Ghana has specific VAT, NHIL, GETFund levies.
   Most imported software forces manual workarounds. We build it in by default.

4/ Language & local context. Business names, Ghanaian bank formats, local
   supplier networks — we design for how business actually works here.

5/ Price. Global SaaS at $200/month is unaffordable for most African SMEs.
   We price in GHS, build lean, and make enterprise tools accessible.

The market is massive. The gap is real. We're filling it.
```
